# Dhammapada

## 1. Yamakavagga ^vagga-1-0

Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,<br>manoseṭṭhā manomayā;<br>Manasā ce paduṭṭhena,<br>bhāsati vā karoti vā;<br>Tato naṁ dukkhamanveti,<br>cakkaṁva vahato padaṁ. ^1

Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,<br>manoseṭṭhā manomayā;<br>Manasā ce pasannena,<br>bhāsati vā karoti vā;<br>Tato naṁ sukhamanveti,<br>chāyāva anapāyinī. ^2

Akkocchi maṁ avadhi maṁ,<br>ajini maṁ ahāsi me;<br>Ye ca taṁ upanayhanti,<br>veraṁ tesaṁ na sammati. ^3

Akkocchi maṁ avadhi maṁ,<br>ajini maṁ ahāsi me;<br>Ye ca taṁ nupanayhanti,<br>veraṁ tesūpasammati. ^4

Na hi verena verāni,<br>sammantīdha kudācanaṁ;<br>Averena ca sammanti,<br>esa dhammo sanantano. ^5

Pare ca na vijānanti,<br>mayamettha yamāmase;<br>Ye ca tattha vijānanti,<br>tato sammanti medhagā. ^6

Subhānupassiṁ viharantaṁ,<br>indriyesu asaṁvutaṁ;<br>Bhojanamhi cāmattaññuṁ,<br>kusītaṁ hīnavīriyaṁ;<br>Taṁ ve pasahati māro,<br>vāto rukkhaṁva dubbalaṁ. ^7

Asubhānupassiṁ viharantaṁ,<br>Indriyesu susaṁvutaṁ;<br>Bhojanamhi ca mattaññuṁ,<br>Saddhaṁ āraddhavīriyaṁ;<br>Taṁ ve nappasahati māro,<br>Vāto selaṁva pabbataṁ. ^8

Anikkasāvo kāsāvaṁ,<br>yo vatthaṁ paridahissati;<br>Apeto damasaccena,<br>na so kāsāvamarahati. ^9

Yo ca vantakasāvassa,<br>sīlesu susamāhito;<br>Upeto damasaccena,<br>sa ve kāsāvamarahati. ^10

Asāre sāramatino,<br>sāre cāsāradassino;<br>Te sāraṁ nādhigacchanti,<br>micchāsaṅkappagocarā. ^11

Sārañca sārato ñatvā,<br>asārañca asārato;<br>Te sāraṁ adhigacchanti,<br>sammāsaṅkappagocarā. ^12

Yathā agāraṁ ducchannaṁ,<br>vuṭṭhī samativijjhati;<br>Evaṁ abhāvitaṁ cittaṁ,<br>rāgo samativijjhati. ^13

Yathā agāraṁ suchannaṁ,<br>vuṭṭhī na samativijjhati;<br>Evaṁ subhāvitaṁ cittaṁ,<br>rāgo na samativijjhati. ^14

Idha socati pecca socati,<br>Pāpakārī ubhayattha socati;<br>So socati so vihaññati,<br>Disvā kammakiliṭṭhamattano. ^15

Idha modati pecca modati,<br>Katapuñño ubhayattha modati;<br>So modati so pamodati,<br>Disvā kammavisuddhimattano. ^16

Idha tappati pecca tappati,<br>Pāpakārī ubhayattha tappati;<br>“Pāpaṁ me katan”ti tappati,<br>Bhiyyo tappati duggatiṁ gato. ^17

Idha nandati pecca nandati,<br>Katapuñño ubhayattha nandati;<br>“Puññaṁ me katan”ti nandati,<br>Bhiyyo nandati suggatiṁ gato. ^18

Bahumpi ce saṁhita bhāsamāno,<br>Na takkaro hoti naro pamatto;<br>Gopova gāvo gaṇayaṁ paresaṁ,<br>Na bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti. ^19

Appampi ce saṁhita bhāsamāno,<br>Dhammassa hoti anudhammacārī;<br>Rāgañca dosañca pahāya mohaṁ,<br>Sammappajāno suvimuttacitto;<br>Anupādiyāno idha vā huraṁ vā,<br>Sa bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti. ^20
