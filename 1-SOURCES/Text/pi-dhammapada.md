# Dhammapada

## 1. Yamakavagga ^vagga-1-0

Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,<br>manoseṭṭhā manomayā;<br>Manasā ce paduṭṭhena,<br>bhāsati vā karoti vā;<br>Tato naṁ dukkhamanveti,<br>cakkaṁva vahato padaṁ. ^1

Manopubbaṅgamā dhammā,<br>manoseṭṭhā manomayā;<br>Manasā ce pasannena,<br>bhāsati vā karoti vā;<br>Tato naṁ sukhamanveti,<br>chāyāva anapāyinī. ^2

Akkocchi maṁ avadhi maṁ,<br>ajini maṁ ahāsi me;<br>Ye ca taṁ upanayhanti,<br>veraṁ tesaṁ na sammati. ^3

Akkocchi maṁ avadhi maṁ,<br>ajini maṁ ahāsi me;<br>Ye ca taṁ nupanayhanti,<br>veraṁ tesūpasammati. ^4

Na hi verena verāni,<br>sammantīdha kudācanaṁ;<br>Averena ca sammanti,<br>esa dhammo sanantano. ^5

Pare ca na vijānanti,<br>mayamettha yamāmase;<br>Ye ca tattha vijānanti,<br>tato sammanti medhagā. ^6

Subhānupassiṁ viharantaṁ,<br>indriyesu asaṁvutaṁ;<br>Bhojanamhi cāmattaññuṁ,<br>kusītaṁ hīnavīriyaṁ;<br>Taṁ ve pasahati māro,<br>vāto rukkhaṁva dubbalaṁ. ^7

Asubhānupassiṁ viharantaṁ,<br>Indriyesu susaṁvutaṁ;<br>Bhojanamhi ca mattaññuṁ,<br>Saddhaṁ āraddhavīriyaṁ;<br>Taṁ ve nappasahati māro,<br>Vāto selaṁva pabbataṁ. ^8

Anikkasāvo kāsāvaṁ,<br>yo vatthaṁ paridahissati;<br>Apeto damasaccena,<br>na so kāsāvamarahati. ^9

Yo ca vantakasāvassa,<br>sīlesu susamāhito;<br>Upeto damasaccena,<br>sa ve kāsāvamarahati. ^10

Asāre sāramatino,<br>sāre cāsāradassino;<br>Te sāraṁ nādhigacchanti,<br>micchāsaṅkappagocarā. ^11

Sārañca sārato ñatvā,<br>asārañca asārato;<br>Te sāraṁ adhigacchanti,<br>sammāsaṅkappagocarā. ^12

Yathā agāraṁ ducchannaṁ,<br>vuṭṭhī samativijjhati;<br>Evaṁ abhāvitaṁ cittaṁ,<br>rāgo samativijjhati. ^13

Yathā agāraṁ suchannaṁ,<br>vuṭṭhī na samativijjhati;<br>Evaṁ subhāvitaṁ cittaṁ,<br>rāgo na samativijjhati. ^14

Idha socati pecca socati,<br>Pāpakārī ubhayattha socati;<br>So socati so vihaññati,<br>Disvā kammakiliṭṭhamattano. ^15

Idha modati pecca modati,<br>Katapuñño ubhayattha modati;<br>So modati so pamodati,<br>Disvā kammavisuddhimattano. ^16

Idha tappati pecca tappati,<br>Pāpakārī ubhayattha tappati;<br>“Pāpaṁ me katan”ti tappati,<br>Bhiyyo tappati duggatiṁ gato. ^17

Idha nandati pecca nandati,<br>Katapuñño ubhayattha nandati;<br>“Puññaṁ me katan”ti nandati,<br>Bhiyyo nandati suggatiṁ gato. ^18

Bahumpi ce saṁhita bhāsamāno,<br>Na takkaro hoti naro pamatto;<br>Gopova gāvo gaṇayaṁ paresaṁ,<br>Na bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti. ^19

Appampi ce saṁhita bhāsamāno,<br>Dhammassa hoti anudhammacārī;<br>Rāgañca dosañca pahāya mohaṁ,<br>Sammappajāno suvimuttacitto;<br>Anupādiyāno idha vā huraṁ vā,<br>Sa bhāgavā sāmaññassa hoti. ^20

## 2. Appamādavagga ^vagga-2-0

Appamādo amatapadaṁ,<br>pamādo maccuno padaṁ;<br>Appamattā na mīyanti,<br>ye pamattā yathā matā. ^21

Evaṁ visesato ñatvā,<br>appamādamhi paṇḍitā;<br>Appamāde pamodanti,<br>ariyānaṁ gocare ratā. ^22

Te jhāyino sātatikā,<br>niccaṁ daḷhaparakkamā;<br>Phusanti dhīrā nibbānaṁ,<br>yogakkhemaṁ anuttaraṁ. ^23

Uṭṭhānavato satīmato,<br>Sucikammassa nisammakārino;<br>Saññatassa dhammajīvino,<br>Appamattassa yasobhivaḍḍhati. ^24

Uṭṭhānenappamādena,<br>saṁyamena damena ca;<br>Dīpaṁ kayirātha medhāvī,<br>yaṁ ogho nābhikīrati. ^25

Pamādamanuyuñjanti,<br>bālā dummedhino janā;<br>Appamādañca medhāvī,<br>dhanaṁ seṭṭhaṁva rakkhati. ^26

Mā pamādamanuyuñjetha,<br>mā kāmaratisanthavaṁ;<br>Appamatto hi jhāyanto,<br>pappoti vipulaṁ sukhaṁ. ^27

Pamādaṁ appamādena,<br>yadā nudati paṇḍito;<br>Paññāpāsādamāruyha,<br>asoko sokiniṁ pajaṁ;<br>Pabbataṭṭhova bhūmaṭṭhe,<br>dhīro bāle avekkhati. ^28

Appamatto pamattesu,<br>suttesu bahujāgaro;<br>Abalassaṁva sīghasso,<br>hitvā yāti sumedhaso. ^29

Appamādena maghavā,<br>devānaṁ seṭṭhataṁ gato;<br>Appamādaṁ pasaṁsanti,<br>pamādo garahito sadā. ^30

Appamādarato bhikkhu,<br>pamāde bhayadassi vā;<br>Saṁyojanaṁ aṇuṁ thūlaṁ,<br>ḍahaṁ aggīva gacchati. ^31

Appamādarato bhikkhu,<br>pamāde bhayadassi vā;<br>Abhabbo parihānāya,<br>nibbānasseva santike. ^32

## 3. Cittavagga ^vagga-3-0

Phandanaṁ capalaṁ cittaṁ,<br>dūrakkhaṁ dunnivārayaṁ;<br>Ujuṁ karoti medhāvī,<br>usukārova tejanaṁ. ^33

Vārijova thale khitto,<br>okamokataubbhato;<br>Pariphandatidaṁ cittaṁ,<br>māradheyyaṁ pahātave. ^34

Dunniggahassa lahuno,<br>yatthakāmanipātino;<br>Cittassa damatho sādhu,<br>cittaṁ dantaṁ sukhāvahaṁ. ^35

Sududdasaṁ sunipuṇaṁ,<br>yatthakāmanipātinaṁ;<br>Cittaṁ rakkhetha medhāvī,<br>cittaṁ guttaṁ sukhāvahaṁ. ^36

Dūraṅgamaṁ ekacaraṁ,<br>asarīraṁ guhāsayaṁ;<br>Ye cittaṁ saṁyamissanti,<br>mokkhanti mārabandhanā. ^37

Anavaṭṭhitacittassa,<br>saddhammaṁ avijānato;<br>Pariplavapasādassa,<br>paññā na paripūrati. ^38

Anavassutacittassa,<br>ananvāhatacetaso;<br>Puññapāpapahīnassa,<br>natthi jāgarato bhayaṁ. ^39

Kumbhūpamaṁ kāyamimaṁ viditvā,<br>Nagarūpamaṁ cittamidaṁ ṭhapetvā;<br>Yodhetha māraṁ paññāvudhena,<br>Jitañca rakkhe anivesano siyā. ^40

Aciraṁ vatayaṁ kāyo,<br>pathaviṁ adhisessati;<br>Chuddho apetaviññāṇo,<br>niratthaṁva kaliṅgaraṁ. ^41

Diso disaṁ yaṁ taṁ kayirā,<br>verī vā pana verinaṁ;<br>Micchāpaṇihitaṁ cittaṁ,<br>pāpiyo naṁ tato kare. ^42

Na taṁ mātā pitā kayirā,<br>aññe vāpi ca ñātakā;<br>Sammāpaṇihitaṁ cittaṁ,<br>seyyaso naṁ tato kare. ^43

## 4. Pupphavagga ^vagga-4-0

Ko imaṁ pathaviṁ vicessati,<br>Yamalokañca imaṁ sadevakaṁ;<br>Ko dhammapadaṁ sudesitaṁ,<br>Kusalo pupphamiva pacessati. ^44

Sekho pathaviṁ vicessati,<br>Yamalokañca imaṁ sadevakaṁ;<br>Sekho dhammapadaṁ sudesitaṁ,<br>Kusalo pupphamiva pacessati. ^45

Pheṇūpamaṁ kāyamimaṁ viditvā,<br>Marīcidhammaṁ abhisambudhāno;<br>Chetvāna mārassa papupphakāni,<br>Adassanaṁ maccurājassa gacche. ^46

Pupphāni heva pacinantaṁ,<br>byāsattamanasaṁ naraṁ;<br>Suttaṁ gāmaṁ mahoghova,<br>maccu ādāya gacchati. ^47

Pupphāni heva pacinantaṁ,<br>byāsattamanasaṁ naraṁ;<br>Atittaññeva kāmesu,<br>antako kurute vasaṁ. ^48

Yathāpi bhamaro pupphaṁ,<br>vaṇṇagandhamaheṭhayaṁ;<br>Paleti rasamādāya,<br>evaṁ gāme munī care. ^49

Na paresaṁ vilomāni,<br>na paresaṁ katākataṁ;<br>Attanova avekkheyya,<br>katāni akatāni ca. ^50

Yathāpi ruciraṁ pupphaṁ,<br>vaṇṇavantaṁ agandhakaṁ;<br>Evaṁ subhāsitā vācā,<br>aphalā hoti akubbato. ^51

Yathāpi ruciraṁ pupphaṁ,<br>Vaṇṇavantaṁ sagandhakaṁ;<br>Evaṁ subhāsitā vācā,<br>Saphalā hoti kubbato. ^52

Yathāpi puppharāsimhā,<br>kayirā mālāguṇe bahū;<br>Evaṁ jātena maccena,<br>kattabbaṁ kusalaṁ bahuṁ. ^53

Na pupphagandho paṭivātameti,<br>Na candanaṁ tagaramallikā vā;<br>Satañca gandho paṭivātameti,<br>Sabbā disā sappuriso pavāyati. ^54

Candanaṁ tagaraṁ vāpi,<br>uppalaṁ atha vassikī;<br>Etesaṁ gandhajātānaṁ,<br>sīlagandho anuttaro. ^55

Appamatto ayaṁ gandho,<br>yāyaṁ tagaracandanī;<br>Yo ca sīlavataṁ gandho,<br>vāti devesu uttamo. ^56

Tesaṁ sampannasīlānaṁ,<br>appamādavihārinaṁ;<br>Sammadaññāvimuttānaṁ,<br>māro maggaṁ na vindati. ^57

Yathā saṅkāradhānasmiṁ,<br>ujjhitasmiṁ mahāpathe;<br>Padumaṁ tattha jāyetha,<br>sucigandhaṁ manoramaṁ. ^58

Evaṁ saṅkārabhūtesu,<br>andhabhūte puthujjane;<br>Atirocati paññāya,<br>sammāsambuddhasāvako. ^59

## 5. Bālavagga ^vagga-5-0

Dīghā jāgarato ratti,<br>dīghaṁ santassa yojanaṁ;<br>Dīgho bālāna saṁsāro,<br>saddhammaṁ avijānataṁ. ^60

Carañce nādhigaccheyya,<br>Seyyaṁ sadisamattano;<br>Ekacariyaṁ daḷhaṁ kayirā,<br>Natthi bāle sahāyatā. ^61

Puttā matthi dhanaṁ matthi,<br>iti bālo vihaññati;<br>Attā hi attano natthi,<br>kuto puttā kuto dhanaṁ. ^62

Yo bālo maññati bālyaṁ,<br>paṇḍito vāpi tena so;<br>Bālo ca paṇḍitamānī,<br>sa ve “bālo”ti vuccati. ^63

Yāvajīvampi ce bālo,<br>paṇḍitaṁ payirupāsati;<br>Na so dhammaṁ vijānāti,<br>dabbī sūparasaṁ yathā. ^64

Muhuttamapi ce viññū,<br>paṇḍitaṁ payirupāsati;<br>Khippaṁ dhammaṁ vijānāti,<br>jivhā sūparasaṁ yathā. ^65

Caranti bālā dummedhā,<br>amitteneva attanā;<br>Karontā pāpakaṁ kammaṁ,<br>yaṁ hoti kaṭukapphalaṁ. ^66

Na taṁ kammaṁ kataṁ sādhu,<br>yaṁ katvā anutappati;<br>Yassa assumukho rodaṁ,<br>vipākaṁ paṭisevati. ^67

Tañca kammaṁ kataṁ sādhu,<br>yaṁ katvā nānutappati;<br>Yassa patīto sumano,<br>vipākaṁ paṭisevati. ^68

Madhuṁvā maññati bālo,<br>yāva pāpaṁ na paccati;<br>Yadā ca paccati pāpaṁ,<br>atha dukkhaṁ nigacchati. ^69

Māse māse kusaggena,<br>bālo bhuñjeyya bhojanaṁ;<br>Na so saṅkhātadhammānaṁ,<br>kalaṁ agghati soḷasiṁ. ^70

Na hi pāpaṁ kataṁ kammaṁ,<br>Sajjukhīraṁva muccati;<br>Ḍahantaṁ bālamanveti,<br>Bhasmacchannova pāvako. ^71

Yāvadeva anatthāya,<br>ñattaṁ bālassa jāyati;<br>Hanti bālassa sukkaṁsaṁ,<br>muddhamassa vipātayaṁ. ^72

Asantaṁ bhāvanamiccheyya,<br>Purekkhārañca bhikkhusu;<br>Āvāsesu ca issariyaṁ,<br>Pūjaṁ parakulesu ca. ^73

Mameva kata maññantu,<br>gihī pabbajitā ubho;<br>Mamevātivasā assu,<br>kiccākiccesu kismici;<br>Iti bālassa saṅkappo,<br>icchā māno ca vaḍḍhati. ^74

Aññā hi lābhūpanisā,<br>aññā nibbānagāminī;<br>Evametaṁ abhiññāya,<br>bhikkhu buddhassa sāvako;<br>Sakkāraṁ nābhinandeyya,<br>vivekamanubrūhaye. ^75

## 6. Paṇḍitavagga ^vagga-6-0

Nidhīnaṁva pavattāraṁ,<br>yaṁ passe vajjadassinaṁ;<br>Niggayhavādiṁ medhāviṁ,<br>tādisaṁ paṇḍitaṁ bhaje;<br>Tādisaṁ bhajamānassa,<br>seyyo hoti na pāpiyo. ^76

Ovadeyyānusāseyya,<br>asabbhā ca nivāraye;<br>Satañhi so piyo hoti,<br>asataṁ hoti appiyo. ^77

Na bhaje pāpake mitte,<br>na bhaje purisādhame;<br>Bhajetha mitte kalyāṇe,<br>bhajetha purisuttame. ^78

Dhammapīti sukhaṁ seti,<br>vippasannena cetasā;<br>Ariyappavedite dhamme,<br>sadā ramati paṇḍito. ^79

Udakañhi nayanti nettikā,<br>Usukārā namayanti tejanaṁ;<br>Dāruṁ namayanti tacchakā,<br>Attānaṁ damayanti paṇḍitā. ^80

Selo yathā ekaghano,<br>vātena na samīrati;<br>Evaṁ nindāpasaṁsāsu,<br>na samiñjanti paṇḍitā. ^81

Yathāpi rahado gambhīro,<br>vippasanno anāvilo;<br>Evaṁ dhammāni sutvāna,<br>vippasīdanti paṇḍitā. ^82

Sabbattha ve sappurisā cajanti,<br>Na kāmakāmā lapayanti santo;<br>Sukhena phuṭṭhā atha vā dukhena,<br>Na uccāvacaṁ paṇḍitā dassayanti. ^83

Na attahetu na parassa hetu,<br>Na puttamicche na dhanaṁ na raṭṭhaṁ;<br>Na iccheyya adhammena samiddhimattano,<br>Sa sīlavā paññavā dhammiko siyā. ^84

Appakā te manussesu,<br>ye janā pāragāmino;<br>Athāyaṁ itarā pajā,<br>tīramevānudhāvati. ^85

Ye ca kho sammadakkhāte,<br>dhamme dhammānuvattino;<br>Te janā pāramessanti,<br>maccudheyyaṁ suduttaraṁ. ^86

Kaṇhaṁ dhammaṁ vippahāya,<br>Sukkaṁ bhāvetha paṇḍito;<br>Okā anokamāgamma,<br>Viveke yattha dūramaṁ. ^87

Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,<br>hitvā kāme akiñcano;<br>Pariyodapeyya attānaṁ,<br>cittaklesehi paṇḍito. ^88

Yesaṁ sambodhiyaṅgesu,<br>sammā cittaṁ subhāvitaṁ;<br>Ādānapaṭinissagge,<br>anupādāya ye ratā;<br>Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,<br>te loke parinibbutā. ^89

## 7. Arahantavagga ^vagga-7-0

Gataddhino visokassa,<br>vippamuttassa sabbadhi;<br>Sabbaganthappahīnassa,<br>pariḷāho na vijjati. ^90

Uyyuñjanti satīmanto,<br>na nikete ramanti te;<br>Haṁsāva pallalaṁ hitvā,<br>okamokaṁ jahanti te. ^91

Yesaṁ sannicayo natthi,<br>ye pariññātabhojanā;<br>Suññato animitto ca,<br>vimokkho yesaṁ gocaro;<br>Ākāseva sakuntānaṁ,<br>gati tesaṁ durannayā. ^92

Yassāsavā parikkhīṇā,<br>āhāre ca anissito;<br>Suññato animitto ca,<br>vimokkho yassa gocaro;<br>Ākāseva sakuntānaṁ,<br>padaṁ tassa durannayaṁ. ^93

Yassindriyāni samathaṅgatāni,<br>Assā yathā sārathinā sudantā;<br>Pahīnamānassa anāsavassa,<br>Devāpi tassa pihayanti tādino. ^94

Pathavisamo no virujjhati,<br>Indakhilupamo tādi subbato;<br>Rahadova apetakaddamo,<br>Saṁsārā na bhavanti tādino. ^95

Santaṁ tassa manaṁ hoti,<br>santā vācā ca kamma ca;<br>Sammadaññāvimuttassa,<br>upasantassa tādino. ^96

Assaddho akataññū ca,<br>sandhicchedo ca yo naro;<br>Hatāvakāso vantāso,<br>sa ve uttamaporiso. ^97

Gāme vā yadi vāraññe,<br>Ninne vā yadi vā thale;<br>Yattha arahanto viharanti,<br>Taṁ bhūmirāmaṇeyyakaṁ. ^98

Ramaṇīyāni araññāni,<br>yattha na ramatī jano;<br>Vītarāgā ramissanti,<br>na te kāmagavesino. ^99

## 8. Sahassavagga ^vagga-8-0

Sahassamapi ce vācā,<br>anatthapadasaṁhitā;<br>Ekaṁ atthapadaṁ seyyo,<br>yaṁ sutvā upasammati. ^100

Sahassamapi ce gāthā,<br>anatthapadasaṁhitā;<br>Ekaṁ gāthāpadaṁ seyyo,<br>yaṁ sutvā upasammati. ^101

Yo ca gāthā sataṁ bhāse,<br>anatthapadasaṁhitā;<br>Ekaṁ dhammapadaṁ seyyo,<br>yaṁ sutvā upasammati. ^102

Yo sahassaṁ sahassena,<br>saṅgāme mānuse jine;<br>Ekañca jeyyamattānaṁ,<br>sa ve saṅgāmajuttamo. ^103

Attā have jitaṁ seyyo,<br>yā cāyaṁ itarā pajā;<br>Attadantassa posassa,<br>niccaṁ saññatacārino. ^104

Neva devo na gandhabbo,<br>na māro saha brahmunā;<br>Jitaṁ apajitaṁ kayirā,<br>tathārūpassa jantuno. ^105

Māse māse sahassena,<br>yo yajetha sataṁ samaṁ;<br>Ekañca bhāvitattānaṁ,<br>muhuttamapi pūjaye;<br>Sāyeva pūjanā seyyo,<br>yañce vassasataṁ hutaṁ. ^106

Yo ca vassasataṁ jantu,<br>aggiṁ paricare vane;<br>Ekañca bhāvitattānaṁ,<br>muhuttamapi pūjaye;<br>Sāyeva pūjanā seyyo,<br>yañce vassasataṁ hutaṁ. ^107

Yaṁ kiñci yiṭṭhaṁ va hutaṁ va loke,<br>Saṁvaccharaṁ yajetha puññapekkho;<br>Sabbampi taṁ na catubhāgameti,<br>Abhivādanā ujjugatesu seyyo. ^108

Abhivādanasīlissa,<br>niccaṁ vuḍḍhāpacāyino;<br>Cattāro dhammā vaḍḍhanti,<br>āyu vaṇṇo sukhaṁ balaṁ. ^109

Yo ca vassasataṁ jīve,<br>dussīlo asamāhito;<br>Ekāhaṁ jīvitaṁ seyyo,<br>sīlavantassa jhāyino. ^110

Yo ca vassasataṁ jīve,<br>duppañño asamāhito;<br>Ekāhaṁ jīvitaṁ seyyo,<br>paññavantassa jhāyino. ^111

Yo ca vassasataṁ jīve,<br>kusīto hīnavīriyo;<br>Ekāhaṁ jīvitaṁ seyyo,<br>vīriyamārabhato daḷhaṁ. ^112

Yo ca vassasataṁ jīve,<br>apassaṁ udayabbayaṁ;<br>Ekāhaṁ jīvitaṁ seyyo,<br>passato udayabbayaṁ. ^113

Yo ca vassasataṁ jīve,<br>apassaṁ amataṁ padaṁ;<br>Ekāhaṁ jīvitaṁ seyyo,<br>passato amataṁ padaṁ. ^114

Yo ca vassasataṁ jīve,<br>apassaṁ dhammamuttamaṁ;<br>Ekāhaṁ jīvitaṁ seyyo,<br>passato dhammamuttamaṁ.<br>Sahassavaggo aṭṭhamo. ^115

## 9. Pāpavagga ^vagga-9-0

Abhittharetha kalyāṇe,<br>pāpā cittaṁ nivāraye;<br>Dandhañhi karoto puññaṁ,<br>pāpasmiṁ ramatī mano. ^116

Pāpañce puriso kayirā,<br>Na naṁ kayirā punappunaṁ;<br>Na tamhi chandaṁ kayirātha,<br>Dukkho pāpassa uccayo. ^117

Puññañce puriso kayirā,<br>kayirā naṁ punappunaṁ;<br>Tamhi chandaṁ kayirātha,<br>sukho puññassa uccayo. ^118

Pāpopi passati bhadraṁ,<br>Yāva pāpaṁ na paccati;<br>Yadā ca paccati pāpaṁ,<br>Atha pāpo pāpāni passati. ^119

Bhadropi passati pāpaṁ,<br>Yāva bhadraṁ na paccati;<br>Yadā ca paccati bhadraṁ,<br>Atha bhadro bhadrāni passati. ^120

Māvamaññetha pāpassa,<br>na mantaṁ āgamissati;<br>Udabindunipātena,<br>udakumbhopi pūrati;<br>Bālo pūrati pāpassa,<br>thokaṁ thokampi ācinaṁ. ^121

Māvamaññetha puññassa,<br>na mandaṁ āgamissati;<br>Udabindunipātena,<br>udakumbhopi pūrati;<br>Dhīro pūrati puññassa,<br>thokaṁ thokampi ācinaṁ. ^122

Vāṇijova bhayaṁ maggaṁ,<br>appasattho mahaddhano;<br>Visaṁ jīvitukāmova,<br>pāpāni parivajjaye. ^123

Pāṇimhi ce vaṇo nāssa,<br>hareyya pāṇinā visaṁ;<br>Nābbaṇaṁ visamanveti,<br>natthi pāpaṁ akubbato. ^124

Yo appaduṭṭhassa narassa dussati,<br>Suddhassa posassa anaṅgaṇassa;<br>Tameva bālaṁ pacceti pāpaṁ,<br>Sukhumo rajo paṭivātaṁva khitto. ^125

Gabbhameke uppajjanti,<br>nirayaṁ pāpakammino;<br>Saggaṁ sugatino yanti,<br>parinibbanti anāsavā. ^126

Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,<br>Na pabbatānaṁ vivaraṁ pavissa;<br>Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,<br>Yatthaṭṭhito mucceyya pāpakammā. ^127

Na antalikkhe na samuddamajjhe,<br>Na pabbatānaṁ vivaraṁ pavissa;<br>Na vijjatī so jagatippadeso,<br>Yatthaṭṭhitaṁ nappasaheyya maccu. ^128

## 10. Daṇḍavagga ^vagga-10-0

Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,<br>sabbe bhāyanti maccuno;<br>Attānaṁ upamaṁ katvā,<br>na haneyya na ghātaye. ^129

Sabbe tasanti daṇḍassa,<br>sabbesaṁ jīvitaṁ piyaṁ;<br>Attānaṁ upamaṁ katvā,<br>na haneyya na ghātaye. ^130

Sukhakāmāni bhūtāni,<br>yo daṇḍena vihiṁsati;<br>Attano sukhamesāno,<br>pecca so na labhate sukhaṁ. ^131

Sukhakāmāni bhūtāni,<br>yo daṇḍena na hiṁsati;<br>Attano sukhamesāno,<br>pecca so labhate sukhaṁ. ^132

Māvoca pharusaṁ kañci,<br>vuttā paṭivadeyyu taṁ;<br>Dukkhā hi sārambhakathā,<br>paṭidaṇḍā phuseyyu taṁ. ^133

Sace neresi attānaṁ,<br>kaṁso upahato yathā;<br>Esa pattosi nibbānaṁ,<br>sārambho te na vijjati. ^134

Yathā daṇḍena gopālo,<br>gāvo pājeti gocaraṁ;<br>Evaṁ jarā ca maccu ca,<br>āyuṁ pājenti pāṇinaṁ. ^135

Atha pāpāni kammāni,<br>karaṁ bālo na bujjhati;<br>Sehi kammehi dummedho,<br>aggidaḍḍhova tappati. ^136

Yo daṇḍena adaṇḍesu,<br>appaduṭṭhesu dussati;<br>Dasannamaññataraṁ ṭhānaṁ,<br>khippameva nigacchati. ^137

Vedanaṁ pharusaṁ jāniṁ,<br>sarīrassa va bhedanaṁ;<br>Garukaṁ vāpi ābādhaṁ,<br>cittakkhepaṁ va pāpuṇe. ^138

Rājato vā upasaggaṁ,<br>Abbhakkhānaṁ va dāruṇaṁ;<br>Parikkhayaṁ va ñātīnaṁ,<br>Bhogānaṁ va pabhaṅguraṁ. ^139

Atha vāssa agārāni,<br>aggi ḍahati pāvako;<br>Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,<br>nirayaṁ sopapajjati. ^140

Na naggacariyā na jaṭā na paṅkā,<br>Nānāsakā thaṇḍilasāyikā vā;<br>Rajojallaṁ ukkuṭikappadhānaṁ,<br>Sodhenti maccaṁ avitiṇṇakaṅkhaṁ. ^141

Alaṅkato cepi samaṁ careyya,<br>Santo danto niyato brahmacārī;<br>Sabbesu bhūtesu nidhāya daṇḍaṁ,<br>So brāhmaṇo so samaṇo sa bhikkhu. ^142

Hirīnisedho puriso,<br>koci lokasmi vijjati;<br>Yo niddaṁ apabodheti,<br>asso bhadro kasāmiva. ^143

Asso yathā bhadro kasāniviṭṭho,<br>Ātāpino saṁvegino bhavātha;<br>Saddhāya sīlena ca vīriyena ca,<br>Samādhinā dhammavinicchayena ca;<br>Sampannavijjācaraṇā patissatā,<br>Jahissatha dukkhamidaṁ anappakaṁ. ^144

Udakañhi nayanti nettikā,<br>Usukārā namayanti tejanaṁ;<br>Dāruṁ namayanti tacchakā,<br>Attānaṁ damayanti subbatā. ^145

## 11. Jarāvagga ^vagga-11-0

Ko nu hāso kimānando,<br>niccaṁ pajjalite sati;<br>Andhakārena onaddhā,<br>padīpaṁ na gavesatha. ^146

Passa cittakataṁ bimbaṁ,<br>arukāyaṁ samussitaṁ;<br>Āturaṁ bahusaṅkappaṁ,<br>yassa natthi dhuvaṁ ṭhiti. ^147

Parijiṇṇamidaṁ rūpaṁ,<br>roganīḷaṁ pabhaṅguraṁ;<br>Bhijjati pūtisandeho,<br>maraṇantañhi jīvitaṁ. ^148

Yānimāni apatthāni,<br>alābūneva sārade;<br>Kāpotakāni aṭṭhīni,<br>tāni disvāna kā rati. ^149

Aṭṭhīnaṁ nagaraṁ kataṁ,<br>maṁsalohitalepanaṁ;<br>Yattha jarā ca maccu ca,<br>māno makkho ca ohito. ^150

Jīranti ve rājarathā sucittā,<br>Atho sarīrampi jaraṁ upeti;<br>Satañca dhammo na jaraṁ upeti,<br>Santo have sabbhi pavedayanti. ^151

Appassutāyaṁ puriso,<br>balībaddhova jīrati;<br>Maṁsāni tassa vaḍḍhanti,<br>paññā tassa na vaḍḍhati. ^152

Anekajātisaṁsāraṁ,<br>sandhāvissaṁ anibbisaṁ;<br>Gahakāraṁ gavesanto,<br>dukkhā jāti punappunaṁ. ^153

Gahakāraka diṭṭhosi,<br>puna gehaṁ na kāhasi;<br>Sabbā te phāsukā bhaggā,<br>gahakūṭaṁ visaṅkhataṁ;<br>Visaṅkhāragataṁ cittaṁ,<br>taṇhānaṁ khayamajjhagā. ^154

Acaritvā brahmacariyaṁ,<br>aladdhā yobbane dhanaṁ;<br>Jiṇṇakoñcāva jhāyanti,<br>khīṇamaccheva pallale. ^155

Acaritvā brahmacariyaṁ,<br>aladdhā yobbane dhanaṁ;<br>Senti cāpātikhīṇāva,<br>purāṇāni anutthunaṁ. ^156

## 12. Attavagga ^vagga-12-0

Attānañce piyaṁ jaññā,<br>rakkheyya naṁ surakkhitaṁ;<br>Tiṇṇaṁ aññataraṁ yāmaṁ,<br>paṭijaggeyya paṇḍito. ^157

Attānameva paṭhamaṁ,<br>patirūpe nivesaye;<br>Athaññamanusāseyya,<br>na kilisseyya paṇḍito. ^158

Attānañce tathā kayirā,<br>yathāññamanusāsati;<br>Sudanto vata dametha,<br>attā hi kira duddamo. ^159

Attā hi attano nātho,<br>ko hi nātho paro siyā;<br>Attanā hi sudantena,<br>nāthaṁ labhati dullabhaṁ. ^160

Attanā hi kataṁ pāpaṁ,<br>Attajaṁ attasambhavaṁ;<br>Abhimatthati dummedhaṁ,<br>Vajiraṁvasmamayaṁ maṇiṁ. ^161

Yassa accantadussilyaṁ,<br>māluvā sālamivotthataṁ;<br>Karoti so tathattānaṁ,<br>yathā naṁ icchatī diso. ^162

Sukarāni asādhūni,<br>attano ahitāni ca;<br>Yaṁ ve hitañca sādhuñca,<br>taṁ ve paramadukkaraṁ. ^163

Yo sāsanaṁ arahataṁ,<br>ariyānaṁ dhammajīvinaṁ;<br>Paṭikkosati dummedho,<br>diṭṭhiṁ nissāya pāpikaṁ;<br>Phalāni kaṭṭhakasseva,<br>attaghātāya phallati. ^164

Attanā hi kataṁ pāpaṁ,<br>attanā saṅkilissati;<br>Attanā akataṁ pāpaṁ,<br>attanāva visujjhati;<br>Suddhī asuddhi paccattaṁ,<br>nāñño aññaṁ visodhaye. ^165

Attadatthaṁ paratthena,<br>bahunāpi na hāpaye;<br>Attadatthamabhiññāya,<br>sadatthapasuto siyā.<br>Attavaggo dvādasamo. ^166

## 13. Lokavagga ^vagga-13-0

Hīnaṁ dhammaṁ na seveyya,<br>pamādena na saṁvase;<br>Micchādiṭṭhiṁ na seveyya,<br>na siyā lokavaḍḍhano. ^167

Uttiṭṭhe nappamajjeyya,<br>dhammaṁ sucaritaṁ care;<br>Dhammacārī sukhaṁ seti,<br>asmiṁ loke paramhi ca. ^168

Dhammaṁ care sucaritaṁ,<br>na naṁ duccaritaṁ care;<br>Dhammacārī sukhaṁ seti,<br>asmiṁ loke paramhi ca. ^169

Yathā pubbuḷakaṁ passe,<br>yathā passe marīcikaṁ;<br>Evaṁ lokaṁ avekkhantaṁ,<br>maccurājā na passati. ^170

Etha passathimaṁ lokaṁ,<br>cittaṁ rājarathūpamaṁ;<br>Yattha bālā visīdanti,<br>natthi saṅgo vijānataṁ. ^171

Yo ca pubbe pamajjitvā,<br>pacchā so nappamajjati;<br>Somaṁ lokaṁ pabhāseti,<br>abbhā muttova candimā. ^172

Yassa pāpaṁ kataṁ kammaṁ,<br>kusalena pidhīyati;<br>Somaṁ lokaṁ pabhāseti,<br>abbhā muttova candimā. ^173

Andhabhūto ayaṁ loko,<br>tanukettha vipassati;<br>Sakuṇo jālamuttova,<br>appo saggāya gacchati. ^174

Haṁsādiccapathe yanti,<br>ākāse yanti iddhiyā;<br>Nīyanti dhīrā lokamhā,<br>jetvā māraṁ savāhiniṁ. ^175

Ekaṁ dhammaṁ atītassa,<br>musāvādissa jantuno;<br>Vitiṇṇaparalokassa,<br>natthi pāpaṁ akāriyaṁ. ^176

Na ve kadariyā devalokaṁ vajanti,<br>Bālā have nappasaṁsanti dānaṁ;<br>Dhīro ca dānaṁ anumodamāno,<br>Teneva so hoti sukhī parattha. ^177

Pathabyā ekarajjena,<br>saggassa gamanena vā;<br>Sabbalokādhipaccena,<br>sotāpattiphalaṁ varaṁ.<br>Lokavaggo terasamo. ^178

## 14. Buddhavagga ^vagga-14-0

Yassa jitaṁ nāvajīyati,<br>Jitaṁ yassa noyāti koci loke;<br>Taṁ buddhamanantagocaraṁ,<br>Apadaṁ kena padena nessatha. ^179

Yassa jālinī visattikā,<br>Taṇhā natthi kuhiñci netave;<br>Taṁ buddhamanantagocaraṁ,<br>Apadaṁ kena padena nessatha. ^180

Ye jhānapasutā dhīrā,<br>nekkhammūpasame ratā;<br>Devāpi tesaṁ pihayanti,<br>sambuddhānaṁ satīmataṁ. ^181

Kiccho manussapaṭilābho,<br>kicchaṁ maccāna jīvitaṁ;<br>Kicchaṁ saddhammassavanaṁ,<br>kiccho buddhānamuppādo. ^182

Sabbapāpassa akaraṇaṁ,<br>kusalassa upasampadā;<br>Sacittapariyodapanaṁ,<br>etaṁ buddhāna sāsanaṁ. ^183

Khantī paramaṁ tapo titikkhā,<br>Nibbānaṁ paramaṁ vadanti buddhā;<br>Na hi pabbajito parūpaghātī,<br>Na samaṇo hoti paraṁ viheṭhayanto. ^184

Anūpavādo anūpaghāto,<br>Pātimokkhe ca saṁvaro;<br>Mattaññutā ca bhattasmiṁ,<br>Pantañca sayanāsanaṁ;<br>Adhicitte ca āyogo,<br>Etaṁ buddhāna sāsanaṁ. ^185

Na kahāpaṇavassena,<br>titti kāmesu vijjati;<br>Appassādā dukhā kāmā,<br>iti viññāya paṇḍito. ^186

Api dibbesu kāmesu,<br>ratiṁ so nādhigacchati;<br>Taṇhakkhayarato hoti,<br>sammāsambuddhasāvako. ^187

Bahuṁ ve saraṇaṁ yanti,<br>pabbatāni vanāni ca;<br>Ārāmarukkhacetyāni,<br>manussā bhayatajjitā. ^188

Netaṁ kho saraṇaṁ khemaṁ,<br>netaṁ saraṇamuttamaṁ;<br>Netaṁ saraṇamāgamma,<br>sabbadukkhā pamuccati. ^189

Yo ca buddhañca dhammañca,<br>saṅghañca saraṇaṁ gato;<br>Cattāri ariyasaccāni,<br>sammappaññāya passati. ^190

Dukkhaṁ dukkhasamuppādaṁ,<br>dukkhassa ca atikkamaṁ;<br>Ariyaṁ caṭṭhaṅgikaṁ maggaṁ,<br>dukkhūpasamagāminaṁ. ^191

Etaṁ kho saraṇaṁ khemaṁ,<br>etaṁ saraṇamuttamaṁ;<br>Etaṁ saraṇamāgamma,<br>sabbadukkhā pamuccati. ^192

Dullabho purisājañño,<br>na so sabbattha jāyati;<br>Yattha so jāyati dhīro,<br>taṁ kulaṁ sukhamedhati. ^193

Sukho buddhānamuppādo,<br>sukhā saddhammadesanā;<br>Sukhā saṅghassa sāmaggī,<br>samaggānaṁ tapo sukho. ^194

Pūjārahe pūjayato,<br>buddhe yadi va sāvake;<br>Papañcasamatikkante,<br>tiṇṇasokapariddave. ^195

Te tādise pūjayato,<br>nibbute akutobhaye;<br>Na sakkā puññaṁ saṅkhātuṁ,<br>imettamapi kenaci. ^196

## 15. Sukhavagga ^vagga-15-0

Susukhaṁ vata jīvāma,<br>verinesu averino;<br>Verinesu manussesu,<br>viharāma averino. ^197

Susukhaṁ vata jīvāma,<br>āturesu anāturā;<br>Āturesu manussesu,<br>viharāma anāturā. ^198

Susukhaṁ vata jīvāma,<br>ussukesu anussukā;<br>Ussukesu manussesu,<br>viharāma anussukā. ^199

Susukhaṁ vata jīvāma,<br>yesaṁ no natthi kiñcanaṁ;<br>Pītibhakkhā bhavissāma,<br>devā ābhassarā yathā. ^200

Jayaṁ veraṁ pasavati,<br>dukkhaṁ seti parājito;<br>Upasanto sukhaṁ seti,<br>hitvā jayaparājayaṁ. ^201

Natthi rāgasamo aggi,<br>Natthi dosasamo kali;<br>Natthi khandhasamā dukkhā,<br>Natthi santiparaṁ sukhaṁ. ^202

Jighacchāparamā rogā,<br>saṅkhāraparamā dukhā;<br>Etaṁ ñatvā yathābhūtaṁ,<br>nibbānaṁ paramaṁ sukhaṁ. ^203

Ārogyaparamā lābhā,<br>Santuṭṭhiparamaṁ dhanaṁ;<br>Vissāsaparamā ñāti,<br>Nibbānaṁ paramaṁ sukhaṁ. ^204

Pavivekarasaṁ pitvā,<br>rasaṁ upasamassa ca;<br>Niddaro hoti nippāpo,<br>dhammapītirasaṁ pivaṁ. ^205

Sāhu dassanamariyānaṁ,<br>sannivāso sadā sukho;<br>Adassanena bālānaṁ,<br>niccameva sukhī siyā. ^206

Bālasaṅgatacārī hi,<br>dīghamaddhāna socati;<br>Dukkho bālehi saṁvāso,<br>amitteneva sabbadā;<br>Dhīro ca sukhasaṁvāso,<br>ñātīnaṁva samāgamo. ^207

Tasmā hi—<br>Dhīrañca paññañca bahussutañca,<br>Dhorayhasīlaṁ vatavantamariyaṁ;<br>Taṁ tādisaṁ sappurisaṁ sumedhaṁ,<br>Bhajetha nakkhattapathaṁva candimā. ^208

## 16. Piyavagga ^vagga-16-0

Ayoge yuñjamattānaṁ,<br>yogasmiñca ayojayaṁ;<br>Atthaṁ hitvā piyaggāhī,<br>pihetattānuyoginaṁ. ^209

Mā piyehi samāgañchi,<br>appiyehi kudācanaṁ;<br>Piyānaṁ adassanaṁ dukkhaṁ,<br>appiyānañca dassanaṁ. ^210

Tasmā piyaṁ na kayirātha,<br>piyāpāyo hi pāpako;<br>Ganthā tesaṁ na vijjanti,<br>yesaṁ natthi piyāppiyaṁ. ^211

Piyato jāyatī soko,<br>piyato jāyatī bhayaṁ;<br>Piyato vippamuttassa,<br>natthi soko kuto bhayaṁ. ^212

Pemato jāyatī soko,<br>pemato jāyatī bhayaṁ;<br>Pemato vippamuttassa,<br>natthi soko kuto bhayaṁ. ^213

Ratiyā jāyatī soko,<br>ratiyā jāyatī bhayaṁ;<br>Ratiyā vippamuttassa,<br>natthi soko kuto bhayaṁ. ^214

Kāmato jāyatī soko,<br>kāmato jāyatī bhayaṁ;<br>Kāmato vippamuttassa,<br>natthi soko kuto bhayaṁ. ^215

Taṇhāya jāyatī soko,<br>taṇhāya jāyatī bhayaṁ;<br>Taṇhāya vippamuttassa,<br>natthi soko kuto bhayaṁ. ^216

Sīladassanasampannaṁ,<br>dhammaṭṭhaṁ saccavedinaṁ;<br>Attano kamma kubbānaṁ,<br>taṁ jano kurute piyaṁ. ^217

Chandajāto anakkhāte,<br>Manasā ca phuṭo siyā;<br>Kāmesu ca appaṭibaddhacitto,<br>Uddhaṁsototi vuccati. ^218

Cirappavāsiṁ purisaṁ,<br>dūrato sotthimāgataṁ;<br>Ñātimittā suhajjā ca,<br>abhinandanti āgataṁ. ^219

Tatheva katapuññampi,<br>asmā lokā paraṁ gataṁ;<br>Puññāni paṭigaṇhanti,<br>piyaṁ ñātīva āgataṁ. ^220

## 17. Kodhavagga ^vagga-17-0

Kodhaṁ jahe vippajaheyya mānaṁ,<br>Saṁyojanaṁ sabbamatikkameyya;<br>Taṁ nāmarūpasmimasajjamānaṁ,<br>Akiñcanaṁ nānupatanti dukkhā. ^221

Yo ve uppatitaṁ kodhaṁ,<br>rathaṁ bhantaṁva vāraye;<br>Tamahaṁ sārathiṁ brūmi,<br>rasmiggāho itaro jano. ^222

Akkodhena jine kodhaṁ,<br>asādhuṁ sādhunā jine;<br>Jine kadariyaṁ dānena,<br>saccenālikavādinaṁ. ^223

Saccaṁ bhaṇe na kujjheyya,<br>dajjā appampi yācito;<br>Etehi tīhi ṭhānehi,<br>gacche devāna santike. ^224

Ahiṁsakā ye munayo,<br>niccaṁ kāyena saṁvutā;<br>Te yanti accutaṁ ṭhānaṁ,<br>yattha gantvā na socare. ^225

Sadā jāgaramānānaṁ,<br>ahorattānusikkhinaṁ;<br>Nibbānaṁ adhimuttānaṁ,<br>atthaṁ gacchanti āsavā. ^226

Porāṇametaṁ atula,<br>netaṁ ajjatanāmiva;<br>Nindanti tuṇhimāsīnaṁ,<br>nindanti bahubhāṇinaṁ;<br>Mitabhāṇimpi nindanti,<br>natthi loke anindito. ^227

Na cāhu na ca bhavissati,<br>na cetarahi vijjati;<br>Ekantaṁ nindito poso,<br>ekantaṁ vā pasaṁsito. ^228

Yañce viññū pasaṁsanti,<br>anuvicca suve suve;<br>Acchiddavuttiṁ medhāviṁ,<br>paññāsīlasamāhitaṁ. ^229

Nikkhaṁ jambonadasseva,<br>ko taṁ ninditumarahati;<br>Devāpi naṁ pasaṁsanti,<br>brahmunāpi pasaṁsito. ^230

Kāyappakopaṁ rakkheyya,<br>kāyena saṁvuto siyā;<br>Kāyaduccaritaṁ hitvā,<br>kāyena sucaritaṁ care. ^231

Vacīpakopaṁ rakkheyya,<br>vācāya saṁvuto siyā;<br>Vacīduccaritaṁ hitvā,<br>vācāya sucaritaṁ care. ^232

Manopakopaṁ rakkheyya,<br>manasā saṁvuto siyā;<br>Manoduccaritaṁ hitvā,<br>manasā sucaritaṁ care. ^233

Kāyena saṁvutā dhīrā,<br>atho vācāya saṁvutā;<br>Manasā saṁvutā dhīrā,<br>te ve suparisaṁvutā. ^234

## 18. Malavagga ^vagga-18-0

Paṇḍupalāsova dānisi,<br>Yamapurisāpi ca te upaṭṭhitā;<br>Uyyogamukhe ca tiṭṭhasi,<br>Pātheyyampi ca te na vijjati. ^235

So karohi dīpamattano,<br>Khippaṁ vāyama paṇḍito bhava;<br>Niddhantamalo anaṅgaṇo,<br>Dibbaṁ ariyabhūmiṁ upehisi. ^236

Upanītavayo ca dānisi,<br>Sampayātosi yamassa santikaṁ;<br>Vāso te natthi antarā,<br>Pātheyyampi ca te na vijjati. ^237

So karohi dīpamattano,<br>Khippaṁ vāyama paṇḍito bhava;<br>Niddhantamalo anaṅgaṇo,<br>Na punaṁ jātijaraṁ upehisi. ^238

Anupubbena medhāvī,<br>thokaṁ thokaṁ khaṇe khaṇe;<br>Kammāro rajatasseva,<br>niddhame malamattano. ^239

Ayasāva malaṁ samuṭṭhitaṁ,<br>Tatuṭṭhāya tameva khādati;<br>Evaṁ atidhonacārinaṁ,<br>Sāni kammāni nayanti duggatiṁ. ^240

Asajjhāyamalā mantā,<br>anuṭṭhānamalā gharā;<br>Malaṁ vaṇṇassa kosajjaṁ,<br>pamādo rakkhato malaṁ. ^241

Malitthiyā duccaritaṁ,<br>maccheraṁ dadato malaṁ;<br>Malā ve pāpakā dhammā,<br>asmiṁ loke paramhi ca. ^242

Tato malā malataraṁ,<br>avijjā paramaṁ malaṁ;<br>Etaṁ malaṁ pahantvāna,<br>nimmalā hotha bhikkhavo. ^243

Sujīvaṁ ahirikena,<br>kākasūrena dhaṁsinā;<br>Pakkhandinā pagabbhena,<br>saṅkiliṭṭhena jīvitaṁ. ^244

Hirīmatā ca dujjīvaṁ,<br>niccaṁ sucigavesinā;<br>Alīnenāppagabbhena,<br>suddhājīvena passatā. ^245

Yo pāṇamatipāteti,<br>musāvādañca bhāsati;<br>Loke adinnamādiyati,<br>paradārañca gacchati. ^246

Surāmerayapānañca,<br>yo naro anuyuñjati;<br>Idheva meso lokasmiṁ,<br>mūlaṁ khaṇati attano. ^247

Evaṁ bho purisa jānāhi,<br>pāpadhammā asaññatā;<br>Mā taṁ lobho adhammo ca,<br>ciraṁ dukkhāya randhayuṁ. ^248

Dadāti ve yathāsaddhaṁ,<br>yathāpasādanaṁ jano;<br>Tattha yo ca maṅku bhavati,<br>paresaṁ pānabhojane;<br>Na so divā vā rattiṁ vā,<br>samādhimadhigacchati. ^249

Yassa cetaṁ samucchinnaṁ,<br>mūlaghaccaṁ samūhataṁ;<br>Sa ve divā vā rattiṁ vā,<br>samādhimadhigacchati. ^250

Natthi rāgasamo aggi,<br>natthi dosasamo gaho;<br>Natthi mohasamaṁ jālaṁ,<br>natthi taṇhāsamā nadī. ^251

Sudassaṁ vajjamaññesaṁ,<br>attano pana duddasaṁ;<br>Paresaṁ hi so vajjāni,<br>opunāti yathā bhusaṁ;<br>Attano pana chādeti,<br>kaliṁva kitavā saṭho. ^252

Paravajjānupassissa,<br>niccaṁ ujjhānasaññino;<br>Āsavā tassa vaḍḍhanti,<br>ārā so āsavakkhayā. ^253

Ākāseva padaṁ natthi,<br>samaṇo natthi bāhire;<br>Papañcābhiratā pajā,<br>nippapañcā tathāgatā. ^254

Ākāseva padaṁ natthi,<br>samaṇo natthi bāhire;<br>Saṅkhārā sassatā natthi,<br>natthi buddhānamiñjitaṁ.<br>Malavaggo aṭṭhārasamo. ^255

## 19. Dhammaṭṭhavagga ^vagga-19-0

Na tena hoti dhammaṭṭho,<br>yenatthaṁ sāhasā naye;<br>Yo ca atthaṁ anatthañca,<br>ubho niccheyya paṇḍito. ^256

Asāhasena dhammena,<br>samena nayatī pare;<br>Dhammassa gutto medhāvī,<br>“dhammaṭṭho”ti pavuccati. ^257

Na tena paṇḍito hoti,<br>yāvatā bahu bhāsati;<br>Khemī averī abhayo,<br>“paṇḍito”ti pavuccati. ^258

Na tāvatā dhammadharo,<br>yāvatā bahu bhāsati;<br>Yo ca appampi sutvāna,<br>dhammaṁ kāyena passati;<br>Sa ve dhammadharo hoti,<br>yo dhammaṁ nappamajjati. ^259

Na tena thero so hoti,<br>yenassa palitaṁ siro;<br>Paripakko vayo tassa,<br>“moghajiṇṇo”ti vuccati. ^260

Yamhi saccañca dhammo ca,<br>ahiṁsā saṁyamo damo;<br>Sa ve vantamalo dhīro,<br>“thero” iti pavuccati. ^261

Na vākkaraṇamattena,<br>vaṇṇapokkharatāya vā;<br>Sādhurūpo naro hoti,<br>issukī maccharī saṭho. ^262

Yassa cetaṁ samucchinnaṁ,<br>mūlaghaccaṁ samūhataṁ;<br>Sa vantadoso medhāvī,<br>“sādhurūpo”ti vuccati. ^263

Na muṇḍakena samaṇo,<br>abbato alikaṁ bhaṇaṁ;<br>Icchālobhasamāpanno,<br>samaṇo kiṁ bhavissati. ^264

Yo ca sameti pāpāni,<br>aṇuṁthūlāni sabbaso;<br>Samitattā hi pāpānaṁ,<br>“samaṇo”ti pavuccati. ^265

Na tena bhikkhu so hoti,<br>yāvatā bhikkhate pare;<br>Vissaṁ dhammaṁ samādāya,<br>bhikkhu hoti na tāvatā. ^266

Yodha puññañca pāpañca,<br>bāhetvā brahmacariyavā;<br>Saṅkhāya loke carati,<br>sa ve “bhikkhū”ti vuccati. ^267

Na monena munī hoti,<br>mūḷharūpo aviddasu;<br>Yo ca tulaṁva paggayha,<br>varamādāya paṇḍito. ^268

Pāpāni parivajjeti,<br>sa munī tena so muni;<br>Yo munāti ubho loke,<br>“muni” tena pavuccati. ^269

Na tena ariyo hoti,<br>yena pāṇāni hiṁsati;<br>Ahiṁsā sabbapāṇānaṁ,<br>“ariyo”ti pavuccati. ^270

Na sīlabbatamattena,<br>bāhusaccena vā pana;<br>Atha vā samādhilābhena,<br>vivittasayanena vā. ^271

Phusāmi nekkhammasukhaṁ,<br>aputhujjanasevitaṁ;<br>Bhikkhu vissāsamāpādi,<br>appatto āsavakkhayaṁ.<br>Dhammaṭṭhavaggo ekūnavīsatimo. ^272

## 20. Maggavagga ^vagga-20-0

Maggānaṭṭhaṅgiko seṭṭho,<br>saccānaṁ caturo padā;<br>Virāgo seṭṭho dhammānaṁ,<br>dvipadānañca cakkhumā. ^273

Eseva maggo natthañño,<br>Dassanassa visuddhiyā;<br>Etañhi tumhe paṭipajjatha,<br>Mārassetaṁ pamohanaṁ. ^274

Etañhi tumhe paṭipannā,<br>dukkhassantaṁ karissatha;<br>Akkhāto vo mayā maggo,<br>aññāya sallakantanaṁ. ^275

Tumhehi kiccamātappaṁ,<br>akkhātāro tathāgatā;<br>Paṭipannā pamokkhanti,<br>jhāyino mārabandhanā. ^276

“Sabbe saṅkhārā aniccā”ti,<br>yadā paññāya passati;<br>Atha nibbindati dukkhe,<br>esa maggo visuddhiyā. ^277

“Sabbe saṅkhārā dukkhā”ti,<br>yadā paññāya passati;<br>Atha nibbindati dukkhe,<br>esa maggo visuddhiyā. ^278

“Sabbe dhammā anattā”ti,<br>yadā paññāya passati;<br>Atha nibbindati dukkhe,<br>esa maggo visuddhiyā. ^279

Uṭṭhānakālamhi anuṭṭhahāno,<br>Yuvā balī ālasiyaṁ upeto;<br>Saṁsannasaṅkappamano kusīto,<br>Paññāya maggaṁ alaso na vindati. ^280

Vācānurakkhī manasā susaṁvuto,<br>Kāyena ca nākusalaṁ kayirā;<br>Ete tayo kammapathe visodhaye,<br>Ārādhaye maggamisippaveditaṁ. ^281

Yogā ve jāyatī bhūri,<br>ayogā bhūrisaṅkhayo;<br>Etaṁ dvedhāpathaṁ ñatvā,<br>bhavāya vibhavāya ca;<br>Tathāttānaṁ niveseyya,<br>yathā bhūri pavaḍḍhati. ^282

Vanaṁ chindatha mā rukkhaṁ,<br>vanato jāyate bhayaṁ;<br>Chetvā vanañca vanathañca,<br>nibbanā hotha bhikkhavo. ^283

Yāva hi vanatho na chijjati,<br>Aṇumattopi narassa nārisu;<br>Paṭibaddhamanova tāva so,<br>Vaccho khīrapakova mātari. ^284

Ucchinda sinehamattano,<br>Kumudaṁ sāradikaṁva pāṇinā;<br>Santimaggameva brūhaya,<br>Nibbānaṁ sugatena desitaṁ. ^285

Idha vassaṁ vasissāmi,<br>idha hemantagimhisu;<br>Iti bālo vicinteti,<br>antarāyaṁ na bujjhati. ^286

Taṁ puttapasusammattaṁ,<br>byāsattamanasaṁ naraṁ;<br>Suttaṁ gāmaṁ mahoghova,<br>maccu ādāya gacchati. ^287

Na santi puttā tāṇāya,<br>na pitā nāpi bandhavā;<br>Antakenādhipannassa,<br>natthi ñātīsu tāṇatā. ^288

Etamatthavasaṁ ñatvā,<br>paṇḍito sīlasaṁvuto;<br>Nibbānagamanaṁ maggaṁ,<br>khippameva visodhaye.<br>Maggavaggo vīsatimo. ^289

## 21. Pakiṇṇakavagga ^vagga-21-0

Mattāsukhapariccāgā,<br>passe ce vipulaṁ sukhaṁ;<br>Caje mattāsukhaṁ dhīro,<br>sampassaṁ vipulaṁ sukhaṁ. ^290

Paradukkhūpadhānena,<br>attano sukhamicchati;<br>Verasaṁsaggasaṁsaṭṭho,<br>verā so na parimuccati. ^291

Yañhi kiccaṁ apaviddhaṁ,<br>akiccaṁ pana karīyati;<br>Unnaḷānaṁ pamattānaṁ,<br>tesaṁ vaḍḍhanti āsavā. ^292

Yesañca susamāraddhā,<br>niccaṁ kāyagatā sati;<br>Akiccaṁ te na sevanti,<br>kicce sātaccakārino;<br>Satānaṁ sampajānānaṁ,<br>atthaṁ gacchanti āsavā. ^293

Mātaraṁ pitaraṁ hantvā,<br>rājāno dve ca khattiye;<br>Raṭṭhaṁ sānucaraṁ hantvā,<br>anīgho yāti brāhmaṇo. ^294

Mātaraṁ pitaraṁ hantvā,<br>rājāno dve ca sotthiye;<br>Veyagghapañcamaṁ hantvā,<br>anīgho yāti brāhmaṇo. ^295

Suppabuddhaṁ pabujjhanti,<br>sadā gotamasāvakā;<br>Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,<br>niccaṁ buddhagatā sati. ^296

Suppabuddhaṁ pabujjhanti,<br>sadā gotamasāvakā;<br>Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,<br>niccaṁ dhammagatā sati. ^297

Suppabuddhaṁ pabujjhanti,<br>sadā gotamasāvakā;<br>Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,<br>niccaṁ saṅghagatā sati. ^298

Suppabuddhaṁ pabujjhanti,<br>sadā gotamasāvakā;<br>Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,<br>niccaṁ kāyagatā sati. ^299

Suppabuddhaṁ pabujjhanti,<br>sadā gotamasāvakā;<br>Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,<br>ahiṁsāya rato mano. ^300

Suppabuddhaṁ pabujjhanti,<br>sadā gotamasāvakā;<br>Yesaṁ divā ca ratto ca,<br>bhāvanāya rato mano. ^301

Duppabbajjaṁ durabhiramaṁ,<br>Durāvāsā gharā dukhā;<br>Dukkhosamānasaṁvāso,<br>Dukkhānupatitaddhagū;<br>Tasmā na caddhagū siyā,<br>Na ca dukkhānupatito siyā. ^302

Saddho sīlena sampanno,<br>yasobhogasamappito;<br>Yaṁ yaṁ padesaṁ bhajati,<br>tattha tattheva pūjito. ^303

Dūre santo pakāsenti,<br>himavantova pabbato;<br>Asantettha na dissanti,<br>rattiṁ khittā yathā sarā. ^304

Ekāsanaṁ ekaseyyaṁ,<br>eko caramatandito;<br>Eko damayamattānaṁ,<br>vanante ramito siyā.<br>Pakiṇṇakavaggo ekavīsatimo. ^305

## 22. Nirayavagga ^vagga-22-0

Abhūtavādī nirayaṁ upeti,<br>Yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha;<br>Ubhopi te pecca samā bhavanti,<br>Nihīnakammā manujā parattha. ^306

Kāsāvakaṇṭhā bahavo,<br>pāpadhammā asaññatā;<br>Pāpā pāpehi kammehi,<br>nirayaṁ te upapajjare. ^307

Seyyo ayoguḷo bhutto,<br>tatto aggisikhūpamo;<br>Yañce bhuñjeyya dussīlo,<br>raṭṭhapiṇḍamasaññato. ^308

Cattāri ṭhānāni naro pamatto,<br>Āpajjati paradārūpasevī;<br>Apuññalābhaṁ na nikāmaseyyaṁ,<br>Nindaṁ tatīyaṁ nirayaṁ catutthaṁ. ^309

Apuññalābho ca gatī ca pāpikā,<br>Bhītassa bhītāya ratī ca thokikā;<br>Rājā ca daṇḍaṁ garukaṁ paṇeti,<br>Tasmā naro paradāraṁ na seve. ^310

Kuso yathā duggahito,<br>hatthamevānukantati;<br>Sāmaññaṁ dupparāmaṭṭhaṁ,<br>nirayāyupakaḍḍhati. ^311

Yaṁ kiñci sithilaṁ kammaṁ,<br>saṅkiliṭṭhañca yaṁ vataṁ;<br>Saṅkassaraṁ brahmacariyaṁ,<br>na taṁ hoti mahapphalaṁ. ^312

Kayirā ce kayirāthenaṁ,<br>daḷhamenaṁ parakkame;<br>Sithilo hi paribbājo,<br>bhiyyo ākirate rajaṁ. ^313

Akataṁ dukkaṭaṁ seyyo,<br>pacchā tappati dukkaṭaṁ;<br>Katañca sukataṁ seyyo,<br>yaṁ katvā nānutappati. ^314

Nagaraṁ yathā paccantaṁ,<br>guttaṁ santarabāhiraṁ;<br>Evaṁ gopetha attānaṁ,<br>khaṇo vo mā upaccagā;<br>Khaṇātītā hi socanti,<br>nirayamhi samappitā. ^315

Alajjitāye lajjanti,<br>lajjitāye na lajjare;<br>Micchādiṭṭhisamādānā,<br>sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ. ^316

Abhaye bhayadassino,<br>bhaye cābhayadassino;<br>Micchādiṭṭhisamādānā,<br>sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ. ^317

Avajje vajjamatino,<br>vajje cāvajjadassino;<br>Micchādiṭṭhisamādānā,<br>sattā gacchanti duggatiṁ. ^318

Vajjañca vajjato ñatvā,<br>avajjañca avajjato;<br>Sammādiṭṭhisamādānā,<br>sattā gacchanti suggatiṁ. ^319

## 23. Nāgavagga ^vagga-23-0

Ahaṁ nāgova saṅgāme,<br>cāpato patitaṁ saraṁ;<br>Ativākyaṁ titikkhissaṁ,<br>dussīlo hi bahujjano. ^320

Dantaṁ nayanti samitiṁ,<br>dantaṁ rājābhirūhati;<br>Danto seṭṭho manussesu,<br>yotivākyaṁ titikkhati. ^321

Varamassatarā dantā,<br>ājānīyā ca sindhavā;<br>Kuñjarā ca mahānāgā,<br>attadanto tato varaṁ. ^322

Na hi etehi yānehi,<br>gaccheyya agataṁ disaṁ;<br>Yathāttanā sudantena,<br>danto dantena gacchati. ^323

Dhanapālo nāma kuñjaro,<br>Kaṭukabhedano dunnivārayo;<br>Baddho kabaḷaṁ na bhuñjati,<br>Sumarati nāgavanassa kuñjaro. ^324

Middhī yadā hoti mahagghaso ca,<br>Niddāyitā samparivattasāyī;<br>Mahāvarāhova nivāpapuṭṭho,<br>Punappunaṁ gabbhamupeti mando. ^325

Idaṁ pure cittamacāri cārikaṁ,<br>Yenicchakaṁ yatthakāmaṁ yathāsukhaṁ;<br>Tadajjahaṁ niggahessāmi yoniso,<br>Hatthippabhinnaṁ viya aṅkusaggaho. ^326

Appamādaratā hotha,<br>sacittamanurakkhatha;<br>Duggā uddharathattānaṁ,<br>paṅke sannova kuñjaro. ^327

Sace labhetha nipakaṁ sahāyaṁ,<br>Saddhiṁ caraṁ sādhuvihāridhīraṁ;<br>Abhibhuyya sabbāni parissayāni,<br>Careyya tenattamano satīmā. ^328

No ce labhetha nipakaṁ sahāyaṁ,<br>Saddhiṁ caraṁ sādhuvihāridhīraṁ;<br>Rājāva raṭṭhaṁ vijitaṁ pahāya,<br>Eko care mātaṅgaraññeva nāgo. ^329

Ekassa caritaṁ seyyo,<br>Natthi bāle sahāyatā;<br>Eko care na ca pāpāni kayirā,<br>Appossukko mātaṅgaraññeva nāgo. ^330

Atthamhi jātamhi sukhā sahāyā,<br>Tuṭṭhī sukhā yā itarītarena;<br>Puññaṁ sukhaṁ jīvitasaṅkhayamhi,<br>Sabbassa dukkhassa sukhaṁ pahānaṁ. ^331

Sukhā matteyyatā loke,<br>atho petteyyatā sukhā;<br>Sukhā sāmaññatā loke,<br>atho brahmaññatā sukhā. ^332

Sukhaṁ yāva jarā sīlaṁ,<br>sukhā saddhā patiṭṭhitā;<br>Sukho paññāya paṭilābho,<br>pāpānaṁ akaraṇaṁ sukhaṁ. ^333

## 24. Taṇhāvagga ^vagga-24-0

Manujassa pamattacārino,<br>Taṇhā vaḍḍhati māluvā viya;<br>So plavatī hurā huraṁ,<br>Phalamicchaṁva vanasmi vānaro. ^334

Yaṁ esā sahate jammī,<br>taṇhā loke visattikā;<br>Sokā tassa pavaḍḍhanti,<br>abhivaṭṭhaṁva bīraṇaṁ. ^335

Yo cetaṁ sahate jammiṁ,<br>taṇhaṁ loke duraccayaṁ;<br>Sokā tamhā papatanti,<br>udabinduva pokkharā. ^336

Taṁ vo vadāmi bhaddaṁ vo,<br>yāvantettha samāgatā;<br>Taṇhāya mūlaṁ khaṇatha,<br>usīratthova bīraṇaṁ;<br>Mā vo naḷaṁva sotova,<br>māro bhañji punappunaṁ. ^337

Yathāpi mūle anupaddave daḷhe,<br>Chinnopi rukkho punareva rūhati;<br>Evampi taṇhānusaye anūhate,<br>Nibbattatī dukkhamidaṁ punappunaṁ. ^338

Yassa chattiṁsati sotā,<br>manāpasavanā bhusā;<br>Mahāvahanti duddiṭṭhiṁ,<br>saṅkappā rāganissitā. ^339

Savanti sabbadhi sotā,<br>latā uppajja tiṭṭhati;<br>Tañca disvā lataṁ jātaṁ,<br>mūlaṁ paññāya chindatha. ^340

Saritāni sinehitāni ca,<br>Somanassāni bhavanti jantuno;<br>Te sātasitā sukhesino,<br>Te ve jātijarūpagā narā. ^341

Tasiṇāya purakkhatā pajā,<br>Parisappanti sasova bandhito;<br>Saṁyojanasaṅgasattakā,<br>Dukkhamupenti punappunaṁ cirāya. ^342

Tasiṇāya purakkhatā pajā,<br>Parisappanti sasova bandhito;<br>Tasmā tasiṇaṁ vinodaye,<br>Ākaṅkhanta virāgamattano. ^343

Yo nibbanatho vanādhimutto,<br>Vanamutto vanameva dhāvati;<br>Taṁ puggalametha passatha,<br>Mutto bandhanameva dhāvati. ^344

Na taṁ daḷhaṁ bandhanamāhu dhīrā,<br>Yadāyasaṁ dārujapabbajañca;<br>Sārattarattā maṇikuṇḍalesu,<br>Puttesu dāresu ca yā apekkhā. ^345

Etaṁ daḷhaṁ bandhanamāhu dhīrā,<br>Ohārinaṁ sithilaṁ duppamuñcaṁ;<br>Etampi chetvāna paribbajanti,<br>Anapekkhino kāmasukhaṁ pahāya. ^346

Ye rāgarattānupatanti sotaṁ,<br>Sayaṅkataṁ makkaṭakova jālaṁ;<br>Etampi chetvāna vajanti dhīrā,<br>Anapekkhino sabbadukkhaṁ pahāya. ^347

Muñca pure muñca pacchato,<br>Majjhe muñca bhavassa pāragū;<br>Sabbattha vimuttamānaso,<br>Na punaṁ jātijaraṁ upehisi. ^348

Vitakkamathitassa jantuno,<br>Tibbarāgassa subhānupassino;<br>Bhiyyo taṇhā pavaḍḍhati,<br>Esa kho daḷhaṁ karoti bandhanaṁ. ^349

Vitakkūpasame ca yo rato,<br>Asubhaṁ bhāvayate sadā sato;<br>Esa kho byanti kāhiti,<br>Esa checchati mārabandhanaṁ. ^350

Niṭṭhaṅgato asantāsī,<br>vītataṇho anaṅgaṇo;<br>Acchindi bhavasallāni,<br>antimoyaṁ samussayo. ^351

Vītataṇho anādāno,<br>Niruttipadakovido;<br>Akkharānaṁ sannipātaṁ,<br>Jaññā pubbāparāni ca;<br>Sa ve “antimasārīro,<br>Mahāpañño mahāpuriso”ti vuccati. ^352

Sabbābhibhū sabbavidūhamasmi,<br>Sabbesu dhammesu anūpalitto;<br>Sabbañjaho taṇhakkhaye vimutto,<br>Sayaṁ abhiññāya kamuddiseyyaṁ. ^353

Sabbadānaṁ dhammadānaṁ jināti,<br>Sabbarasaṁ dhammaraso jināti;<br>Sabbaratiṁ dhammarati jināti,<br>Taṇhakkhayo sabbadukkhaṁ jināti. ^354

Hananti bhogā dummedhaṁ,<br>no ca pāragavesino;<br>Bhogataṇhāya dummedho,<br>hanti aññeva attanaṁ. ^355

Tiṇadosāni khettāni,<br>rāgadosā ayaṁ pajā;<br>Tasmā hi vītarāgesu,<br>dinnaṁ hoti mahapphalaṁ. ^356

Tiṇadosāni khettāni,<br>dosadosā ayaṁ pajā;<br>Tasmā hi vītadosesu,<br>dinnaṁ hoti mahapphalaṁ. ^357

Tiṇadosāni khettāni,<br>mohadosā ayaṁ pajā;<br>Tasmā hi vītamohesu,<br>dinnaṁ hoti mahapphalaṁ. ^358

Tiṇadosāni khettāni,<br>icchādosā ayaṁ pajā;<br>Tasmā hi vigaticchesu,<br>dinnaṁ hoti mahapphalaṁ. ^359

## 25. Bhikkhuvagga ^vagga-25-0

Cakkhunā saṁvaro sādhu,<br>sādhu sotena saṁvaro;<br>Ghānena saṁvaro sādhu,<br>sādhu jivhāya saṁvaro. ^360

Kāyena saṁvaro sādhu,<br>sādhu vācāya saṁvaro;<br>Manasā saṁvaro sādhu,<br>sādhu sabbattha saṁvaro;<br>Sabbattha saṁvuto bhikkhu,<br>sabbadukkhā pamuccati. ^361

Hatthasaṁyato pādasaṁyato,<br>Vācāsaṁyato saṁyatuttamo;<br>Ajjhattarato samāhito,<br>Eko santusito tamāhu bhikkhuṁ. ^362

Yo mukhasaṁyato bhikkhu,<br>mantabhāṇī anuddhato;<br>Atthaṁ dhammañca dīpeti,<br>madhuraṁ tassa bhāsitaṁ. ^363

Dhammārāmo dhammarato,<br>dhammaṁ anuvicintayaṁ;<br>Dhammaṁ anussaraṁ bhikkhu,<br>saddhammā na parihāyati. ^364

Salābhaṁ nātimaññeyya,<br>nāññesaṁ pihayaṁ care;<br>Aññesaṁ pihayaṁ bhikkhu,<br>samādhiṁ nādhigacchati. ^365

Appalābhopi ce bhikkhu,<br>salābhaṁ nātimaññati;<br>Taṁ ve devā pasaṁsanti,<br>suddhājīviṁ atanditaṁ. ^366

Sabbaso nāmarūpasmiṁ,<br>yassa natthi mamāyitaṁ;<br>Asatā ca na socati,<br>sa ve “bhikkhū”ti vuccati. ^367

Mettāvihārī yo bhikkhu,<br>pasanno buddhasāsane;<br>Adhigacche padaṁ santaṁ,<br>saṅkhārūpasamaṁ sukhaṁ. ^368

Siñca bhikkhu imaṁ nāvaṁ,<br>sittā te lahumessati;<br>Chetvā rāgañca dosañca,<br>tato nibbānamehisi. ^369

Pañca chinde pañca jahe,<br>pañca cuttari bhāvaye;<br>Pañca saṅgātigo bhikkhu,<br>“oghatiṇṇo”ti vuccati. ^370

Jhāya bhikkhu mā pamādo,<br>Mā te kāmaguṇe ramessu cittaṁ;<br>Mā lohaguḷaṁ gilī pamatto,<br>Mā kandi “dukkhamidan”ti dayhamāno. ^371

Natthi jhānaṁ apaññassa,<br>paññā natthi ajhāyato;<br>Yamhi jhānañca paññā ca,<br>sa ve nibbānasantike. ^372

Suññāgāraṁ paviṭṭhassa,<br>santacittassa bhikkhuno;<br>Amānusī rati hoti,<br>sammā dhammaṁ vipassato. ^373

Yato yato sammasati,<br>khandhānaṁ udayabbayaṁ;<br>Labhatī pītipāmojjaṁ,<br>amataṁ taṁ vijānataṁ. ^374

Tatrāyamādi bhavati,<br>idha paññassa bhikkhuno;<br>Indriyagutti santuṭṭhi,<br>pātimokkhe ca saṁvaro. ^375

Mitte bhajassu kalyāṇe,<br>suddhājīve atandite;<br>Paṭisanthāravutyassa,<br>ācārakusalo siyā;<br>Tato pāmojjabahulo,<br>dukkhassantaṁ karissati. ^376

Vassikā viya pupphāni,<br>maddavāni pamuñcati;<br>Evaṁ rāgañca dosañca,<br>vippamuñcetha bhikkhavo. ^377

Santakāyo santavāco,<br>santavā susamāhito;<br>Vantalokāmiso bhikkhu,<br>“upasanto”ti vuccati. ^378

Attanā codayattānaṁ,<br>paṭimaṁsetha attanā;<br>So attagutto satimā,<br>sukhaṁ bhikkhu vihāhisi. ^379

Attā hi attano nātho,<br>ko hi nātho paro siyā;<br>Attā hi attano gati,<br>tasmā saṁyamamattānaṁ;<br>Assaṁ bhadraṁva vāṇijo. ^380

Pāmojjabahulo bhikkhu,<br>pasanno buddhasāsane;<br>Adhigacche padaṁ santaṁ,<br>saṅkhārūpasamaṁ sukhaṁ. ^381

Yo have daharo bhikkhu,<br>yuñjati buddhasāsane;<br>Somaṁ lokaṁ pabhāseti,<br>abbhā muttova candimā. ^382

## 26. Brāhmaṇavagga ^vagga-26-0

Chinda sotaṁ parakkamma,<br>kāme panuda brāhmaṇa;<br>Saṅkhārānaṁ khayaṁ ñatvā,<br>akataññūsi brāhmaṇa. ^383

Yadā dvayesu dhammesu,<br>pāragū hoti brāhmaṇo;<br>Athassa sabbe saṁyogā,<br>atthaṁ gacchanti jānato. ^384

Yassa pāraṁ apāraṁ vā,<br>pārāpāraṁ na vijjati;<br>Vītaddaraṁ visaṁyuttaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^385

Jhāyiṁ virajamāsīnaṁ,<br>katakiccamanāsavaṁ;<br>Uttamatthamanuppattaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^386

Divā tapati ādicco,<br>rattimābhāti candimā;<br>Sannaddho khattiyo tapati,<br>jhāyī tapati brāhmaṇo;<br>Atha sabbamahorattiṁ,<br>buddho tapati tejasā. ^387

Bāhitapāpoti brāhmaṇo,<br>Samacariyā samaṇoti vuccati;<br>Pabbājayamattano malaṁ,<br>Tasmā “pabbajito”ti vuccati. ^388

Na brāhmaṇassa pahareyya,<br>nāssa muñcetha brāhmaṇo;<br>Dhī brāhmaṇassa hantāraṁ,<br>tato dhī yassa muñcati. ^389

Na brāhmaṇassetadakiñci seyyo,<br>Yadā nisedho manaso piyehi;<br>Yato yato hiṁsamano nivattati,<br>Tato tato sammatimeva dukkhaṁ. ^390

Yassa kāyena vācāya,<br>manasā natthi dukkaṭaṁ;<br>Saṁvutaṁ tīhi ṭhānehi,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^391

Yamhā dhammaṁ vijāneyya,<br>sammāsambuddhadesitaṁ;<br>Sakkaccaṁ taṁ namasseyya,<br>aggihuttaṁva brāhmaṇo. ^392

Na jaṭāhi na gottena,<br>na jaccā hoti brāhmaṇo;<br>Yamhi saccañca dhammo ca,<br>so sucī so ca brāhmaṇo. ^393

Kiṁ te jaṭāhi dummedha,<br>kiṁ te ajinasāṭiyā;<br>Abbhantaraṁ te gahanaṁ,<br>bāhiraṁ parimajjasi. ^394

Paṁsukūladharaṁ jantuṁ,<br>kisaṁ dhamanisanthataṁ;<br>Ekaṁ vanasmiṁ jhāyantaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^395

Na cāhaṁ brāhmaṇaṁ brūmi,<br>yonijaṁ mattisambhavaṁ;<br>Bhovādi nāma so hoti,<br>sace hoti sakiñcano;<br>Akiñcanaṁ anādānaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^396

Sabbasaṁyojanaṁ chetvā,<br>yo ve na paritassati;<br>Saṅgātigaṁ visaṁyuttaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^397

Chetvā naddhiṁ varattañca,<br>sandānaṁ sahanukkamaṁ;<br>Ukkhittapalighaṁ buddhaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^398

Akkosaṁ vadhabandhañca,<br>aduṭṭho yo titikkhati;<br>Khantībalaṁ balānīkaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^399

Akkodhanaṁ vatavantaṁ,<br>sīlavantaṁ anussadaṁ;<br>Dantaṁ antimasārīraṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^400

Vāri pokkharapatteva,<br>āraggeriva sāsapo;<br>Yo na limpati kāmesu,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^401

Yo dukkhassa pajānāti,<br>idheva khayamattano;<br>Pannabhāraṁ visaṁyuttaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^402

Gambhīrapaññaṁ medhāviṁ,<br>maggāmaggassa kovidaṁ;<br>Uttamatthamanuppattaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^403

Asaṁsaṭṭhaṁ gahaṭṭhehi,<br>anāgārehi cūbhayaṁ;<br>Anokasārimappicchaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^404

Nidhāya daṇḍaṁ bhūtesu,<br>tasesu thāvaresu ca;<br>Yo na hanti na ghāteti,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^405

Aviruddhaṁ viruddhesu,<br>attadaṇḍesu nibbutaṁ;<br>Sādānesu anādānaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^406

Yassa rāgo ca doso ca,<br>māno makkho ca pātito;<br>Sāsaporiva āraggā,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^407

Akakkasaṁ viññāpaniṁ,<br>giraṁ saccamudīraye;<br>Yāya nābhisaje kañci,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^408

Yodha dīghaṁ va rassaṁ vā,<br>aṇuṁ thūlaṁ subhāsubhaṁ;<br>Loke adinnaṁ nādiyati,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^409

Āsā yassa na vijjanti,<br>asmiṁ loke paramhi ca;<br>Nirāsāsaṁ visaṁyuttaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^410

Yassālayā na vijjanti,<br>aññāya akathaṅkathī;<br>Amatogadhamanuppattaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^411

Yodha puññañca pāpañca,<br>ubho saṅgamupaccagā;<br>Asokaṁ virajaṁ suddhaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^412

Candaṁva vimalaṁ suddhaṁ,<br>vippasannamanāvilaṁ;<br>Nandībhavaparikkhīṇaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^413

Yomaṁ palipathaṁ duggaṁ,<br>saṁsāraṁ mohamaccagā;<br>Tiṇṇo pāraṅgato jhāyī,<br>anejo akathaṅkathī;<br>Anupādāya nibbuto,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^414

Yodha kāme pahantvāna,<br>anāgāro paribbaje;<br>Kāmabhavaparikkhīṇaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^415

Yodha taṇhaṁ pahantvāna,<br>anāgāro paribbaje;<br>Taṇhābhavaparikkhīṇaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ.<br>Yodha taṇhaṁ pahantvāna,<br>anāgāro paribbaje;<br>Taṇhābhavaparikkhīṇaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^416

Hitvā mānusakaṁ yogaṁ,<br>dibbaṁ yogaṁ upaccagā;<br>Sabbayogavisaṁyuttaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^417

Hitvā ratiñca aratiñca,<br>sītibhūtaṁ nirūpadhiṁ;<br>Sabbalokābhibhuṁ vīraṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^418

Cutiṁ yo vedi sattānaṁ,<br>upapattiñca sabbaso;<br>Asattaṁ sugataṁ buddhaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^419

Yassa gatiṁ na jānanti,<br>devā gandhabbamānusā;<br>Khīṇāsavaṁ arahantaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^420

Yassa pure ca pacchā ca,<br>majjhe ca natthi kiñcanaṁ;<br>Akiñcanaṁ anādānaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^421

Usabhaṁ pavaraṁ vīraṁ,<br>mahesiṁ vijitāvinaṁ;<br>Anejaṁ nhātakaṁ buddhaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ. ^422

Pubbenivāsaṁ yo vedi,<br>saggāpāyañca passati;<br>Atho jātikkhayaṁ patto,<br>abhiññāvosito muni;<br>Sabbavositavosānaṁ,<br>tamahaṁ brūmi brāhmaṇaṁ.<br>Dhammapade nipātamhi,<br>Dhammapadapāḷi samattā. ^423
