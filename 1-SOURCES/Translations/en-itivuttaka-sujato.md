This was said by the Buddha, the Perfected One: that is what I heard. ^iti22-1-1

“Mendicants, don’t fear good deeds. ^iti22-2-1

giving, self-control, and restraint.’” ^iti22-4-5

developing a mind of love: ^iti22-5-4
