# The Single Stanza

> **84000 English, stored verbatim as a reference layer** (CC BY-NC-ND — do not excerpt or adapt for output; the shipped verse is our own translation, citing 84000). Tibetan source: `1-SOURCES/Text/bo-toh323.md`. Folio anchors `^F<folio>` align to the Degé Kangyur.

Homage to the Three Jewels! [n1] ^F204a

Foremost among humans, there is no other great ascetic like you,<br>Neither in the heavenly lands, in this world, in Vaiśravaṇa’s abode,<br>In the excellent abodes of the gods’ palaces, nor in the cardinal and intermediate directions.<br>How could there be another one like you on this vast earth with its mountains and forests?


## Notes (84000)

[n1] Kano’s Sanskrit edition (2011): oṃ namo buddhāya. “Oṃ! Homage to the Buddha!”
