# Day 44 — Patience beautifies (The Inquiry of Jayamati, Toh 194)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/toh194-patience]] · **own translation from the Degé Kangyur (Public Domain)**; 84000 English as reference only. A Kangyur Mahāyāna sūtra.
Short aphorism, **quoted in full**. Meaning: patience makes one lovely; anger disfigures.

## Source (Tibetan, Toh 194 — verbatim)

> གཟུགས་བཟང་བ་འདོད་པས་བཟོད་པ་བསྒོམ་པར་བྱའོ། (`bo-toh194.md#^p502`)
