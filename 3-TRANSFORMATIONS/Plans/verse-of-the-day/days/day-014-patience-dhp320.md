# Day 14 — Patience (Dhp 320)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/dhp-320]] · grounded in Sujato (CC0).
Short single verse, **translated in full**. Meaning: as a war-elephant endures the arrows shot at it in battle, so I will patiently endure harsh words, for many people behave badly. Steadiness under provocation (*khanti* / *titikkhā*), distinct from Day 7's active non-retaliation.
