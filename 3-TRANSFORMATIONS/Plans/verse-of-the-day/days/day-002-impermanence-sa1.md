# Verse of the Day — Impermanence

**Source:** [[2-RAILS/Verses/sa-1.md|SĀ 1]] · **Theme:** impermanence · **Speaks to:** grief, craving, letting go (equanimity)

> SĀ 1 is a short prose discourse; per the rail, the daily card distils its core chain — *impermanence → seeing clearly → craving fades → the heart is freed* — rather than the whole sutta.

## Source (Literary Chinese)

> 當觀色無常。如是觀者，則為正觀。正觀者，則生厭離；厭離者，喜貪盡；喜貪盡者，說心解脫。

## Renderings

**English** *(meaning anchored on Charles Patton, CC0)*
> Contemplate body and mind as impermanent. Seeing this rightly, fascination fades; and as craving fades, the heart is set free.
