# Day 16 — Peace of a Good Heart (MĀ 16)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/ma-16]] · grounded in Patton (CC0); CBETA source CC BY-NC-SA.
**Note (distillation):** MĀ 16 (the Chinese Kālāmas discourse) is a long prose sutta with no verse form. The card renders its **own repeated conclusion** — the "four peaceful dwelling places," i.e. the present-life ease of a heart without ill-will — condensed to the fourth solace. Faithful to the sutta's summary, but a heavy distillation; flagged per the "prefer short verses" rule (same situation as the swapped SĀ 33; a Chinese short-verse source is still owed).
**Do not** import the famous Pali "do not go by tradition/report" free-inquiry framing — it is not in this Chinese source.

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — With a heart free of ill-will, you live at peace here and now, without enemies and with nothing to fear.
- **zh** — 心無瞋恚，當下即安住於無怨、無畏之中;不作惡，苦從何來？
- **bo** — ⚑ སེམས་ལ་ཞེ་སྡང་མེད་ན། ད་ལྟ་ཉིད་དུ་འཁོན་པ་མེད་ཅིང་འཇིགས་པ་མེད་པར་བདེ་བར་གནས།
- **hi** — द्वेषरहित मन से तुम यहीं और अभी शांति में रहते हो, बिना किसी शत्रु के और बिना किसी भय के।
- **ne** — द्वेषरहित मनले तिमी यहीँ र अहिले शान्तिमा बस्छौ, कुनै शत्रु बिना र कुनै भय बिना।
- **mn** — ⚑ Хорсолгүй сэтгэлтэй бол чи яг одоо, дайсангүй, айдасгүй амар амгалан оршино.
