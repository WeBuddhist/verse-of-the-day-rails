# Day 4 — Self-mastery (法句經 T210, 述千品)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t210-self-conquest]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); meaning grounded on the **Dhp 103** parallel (Sujato, CC0). Replaces the earlier SĀ 379 card (no compliant single-sentence quote in that source).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: victory over a thousand thousand foes is less than victory over oneself.

## Source (Chinese, T210 — verbatim)

> 千千為敵，一夫勝之，未若自勝，為戰中上。 (`zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0564b1901`)
