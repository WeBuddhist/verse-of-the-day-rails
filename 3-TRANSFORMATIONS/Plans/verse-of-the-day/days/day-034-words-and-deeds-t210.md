# Day 34 — Words and deeds (法句經 T210, 華香品)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t210-words-deeds]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); meaning grounded on the **Dhp 51** parallel (Sujato, CC0).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: like a lovely but scentless flower, fine words bear no fruit for one who does not act on them.

## Source (Chinese, T210 — verbatim)

> 如可意華，色好無香，工語如是，不行無得。 (`zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0563a2301`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — Like a lovely flower without scent, fine words bear no fruit for one who does not act on them.
- **zh** — 如可意華，色好無香，工語如是，不行無得。
