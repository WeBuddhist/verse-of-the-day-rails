# Day 67 — Inner peace (Ekottarika Āgama, 卷6)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/ea-mind-not-sick]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: even when the body is ill, the mind need not fall ill with it.

## Source (Chinese, EĀ 卷6 — verbatim)

> 雖身有病，令心無病。 (`zh-ekottarika-agama.md#^pT02p0573a0516`)
