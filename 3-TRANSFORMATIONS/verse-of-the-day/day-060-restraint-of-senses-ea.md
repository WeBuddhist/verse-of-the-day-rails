# Day 60 — Restraint of the senses (Ekottarika Āgama, the six sense-contacts)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/ea-restraint]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: when the senses are unguarded, clinging arises that will not let go and keeps one bound to birth and death.

## Source (Chinese, EĀ — verbatim)

> 凡夫之人，若眼見色，便起染著之心，不能捨離；彼以見色，極起愛著，流轉生死，無有解時。 (`zh-ekottarika-agama.md#^pT02p0728b0202`)
