# Day 58 — Wholesome action (Dīrgha Āgama 8, 散陀那經)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/da-8-wholesome-action]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: the whole aim of the teaching is to end what is unwholesome and grow what is wholesome.

## Source (Chinese, DĀ 8 — verbatim)

> 吾所說法，微妙第一，為滅不善，增益善法。 (`zh-digha-agama.md#^pT01p0049b0401`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — The teaching I give is subtle and supreme: to end what is unwholesome and grow what is wholesome.
