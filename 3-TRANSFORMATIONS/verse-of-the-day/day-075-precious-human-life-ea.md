# Day 75 — Precious human life (Ekottarika Āgama, 卷38)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/ea-buddhas-among-humans]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: awakening is realized in a human life, not in the heavens; this human life is the ground of the path.

## Source (Chinese, EĀ 卷38 — verbatim)
