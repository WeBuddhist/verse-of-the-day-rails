# Day 62 — True offering is practice (Dīrgha Āgama 2, 遊行經)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/da-2-true-offering]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: the truest offering to the Buddha is to receive the teaching and live it.

## Source (Chinese, DĀ 2 — verbatim)

> 人能受法，能行法者，斯乃名曰供養如來。 (`zh-digha-agama.md#^pT01p0021a1103`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — One who receives the teaching and puts it into practice: this is the true offering to the Buddha.
- **zh** — 人能受法，能行法者，斯乃名曰供養如來。
- **bo** — ⚑ གང་ཞིག་ཆོས་ཐོས་ནས་ལག་ལེན་དུ་འགེབས་པ་དེ་ནི་དེ་བཞིན་གཤེགས་པ་ལ་མཆོད་པ་ཞེས་བྱའོ། །

- **ne** — जसले धर्म ग्रहण गरी त्यसको आचरण गर्छ, त्यही नै तथागतको साँचो पूजा हो।
