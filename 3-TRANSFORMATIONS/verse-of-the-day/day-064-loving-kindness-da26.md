# Day 64 — Loving-kindness (Dīrgha Āgama 26, 三明經)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/da-26-loving-kindness]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: a heart cultivated in loving-kindness carries one swiftly to a happy state.

## Source (Chinese, DĀ 26 — verbatim)

> 當知行慈比丘身壞命終，如發箭之頃，生梵天上。 (`zh-digha-agama.md#^pT01p0107a1008`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — One who lives with a heart of loving-kindness, when this life ends, is reborn in a heavenly realm as swiftly as a loosed arrow.
