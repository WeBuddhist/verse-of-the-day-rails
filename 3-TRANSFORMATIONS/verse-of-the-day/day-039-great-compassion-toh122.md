# Day 39 — Great compassion (Wisdom at the Hour of Death, Toh 122)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/toh122-great-compassion]] · **own translation from the Degé Kangyur (Public Domain)**; 84000 English as reference only (CC BY-NC-ND). A **Tibetan Kangyur Mahāyāna sūtra** (not the Udānavarga).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: grounded in bodhicitta, cultivate great compassion.

## Source (Tibetan, Toh 122 — verbatim)

> བྱང་ཆུབ་སེམས་དང་རབ་ལྡན་པས། །སྙིང་རྗེ་ཆེན་པོའི་འདུ་ཤེས་བསྒོམ། (`bo-toh122.md#^p307`)
