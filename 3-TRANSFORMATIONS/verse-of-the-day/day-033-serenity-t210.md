# Day 33 — Serenity of the wise (法句經 T210, 明哲品)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t210-deep-lake]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); meaning grounded on the **Dhp 82** parallel (Sujato, CC0).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: like a deep, clear lake, the wise grow serene on hearing the teaching.

## Source (Chinese, T210 — verbatim)

> 譬如深淵，澄靜清明，慧人聞道，心淨歡然。 (`zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0564a0101`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **zh** — 譬如深淵，澄靜清明，慧人聞道，心淨歡然。
