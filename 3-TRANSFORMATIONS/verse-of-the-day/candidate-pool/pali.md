**Note on the "three marks" triad (Dhp 277-279):** each verse restates the same structure with a different mark (aniccā/dukkhā/anattā) — a clean parallel to the Chinese `four-dharma-seals` candidate already in `chinese.md` (EA juan 36), but the Pali tradition's own threefold framing (no explicit "nirvana as cessation" clause). Worth keeping distinct — a future selection could use either or both across different days.
