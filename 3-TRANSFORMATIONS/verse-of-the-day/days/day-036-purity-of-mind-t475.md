# Day 36 — Purity of mind (維摩詰所說經 T475)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t475-purity-of-mind]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); Mahāyāna. Meaning cross-checked against standard Vimalakīrti translations.
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: purify the mind, and as the mind becomes pure, so one's world becomes pure.

## Source (Chinese, T475 — verbatim)

> 當淨其心；隨其心淨，則佛土淨。 (`zh-vimalakirti.md#^pT14p0538b2609`)
