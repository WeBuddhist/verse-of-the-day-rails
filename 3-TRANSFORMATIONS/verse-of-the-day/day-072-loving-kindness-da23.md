# Day 72 — Loving-kindness (Dīrgha Āgama 23, 究羅檀頭經)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/da-23-loving-kindness]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: even a brief moment of loving-kindness for all beings is the most worthwhile act of all.

## Source (Chinese, DĀ 23 — verbatim)

> 若能以慈心念一切眾生，如搆牛乳頃，其福最勝。 (`zh-digha-agama.md#^pT01p0100c1716`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — To hold all beings in loving-kindness, even for as long as it takes to milk a cow, is the most supreme merit of all.
- **zh** — 若能以慈心念一切眾生，如搆牛乳頃，其福最勝。

- **ne** — यदि कसैले सबै प्राणीलाई मैत्री-चित्तले, गाई दुहुने क्षणभर पनि सम्झन्छ भने, उसको पुण्य सर्वोत्तम हुन्छ।
