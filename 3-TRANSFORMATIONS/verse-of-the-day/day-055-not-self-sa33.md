# Day 55 — Not Self (SĀ 33)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/sa-33]] · grounded in Patton (CC0); CBETA source CC BY-NC-SA. Own translation direct from the Chinese source.
Short sentence-chain, **quoted in full** (one contiguous run of the source, stopping before the closing arahant-declaration formula). Meaning: seeing the five aggregates as not-self leads, step by step, to release.

## Source (Literary Chinese — verbatim)

> 多聞聖弟子於此五受陰非我、非我所，如實觀察。如實觀察已，於諸世間都無所取，無所取故無所著，無所著故自覺涅槃。 (`zh-samyukta-agama.md#^pT02p0007c0704`, within `^sa33-0`)
