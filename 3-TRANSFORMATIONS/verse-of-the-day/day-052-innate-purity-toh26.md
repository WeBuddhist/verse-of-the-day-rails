# Day 52 — Innate purity (Sūryagarbha Perfection of Wisdom, Toh 26)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/toh26-innate-purity]] · **own translation from the Degé Kangyur (Public Domain)**; 84000 English as reference only (CC BY-NC-ND). A **Tibetan Kangyur Mahāyāna sūtra** (Prajñāpāramitā, not the Udānavarga).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: all things are, by their nature, already pure — practice is recognizing this, not manufacturing it.

## Source (Tibetan, Toh 26 — verbatim)

> རིགས་ཀྱི་བུ་ཆོས་ཐམས་ཅད་རང་བཞིན་གྱིས་རྣམ་པར་དག་པ་ལ་བསླབ་པར་བྱའོ། (`bo-toh26.md#^p354`)
