# Day 70 — Great compassion (Ekottarika Āgama, 卷5)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/ea-tend-the-sick]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source (CC BY-NC-SA)**; a **Chinese Āgama** discourse (zh IS the verbatim source).
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: to care for a sick person is to care for the Buddha himself.

## Source (Chinese, EĀ 卷5 — verbatim)

> 其有瞻視病者，則為瞻視我已。 (`zh-ekottarika-agama.md#^pT02p0569c0102`)
