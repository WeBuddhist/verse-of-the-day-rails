# Day 28 — Small good deeds (法句經 T210, 惡行品)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t210-small-good]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); meaning grounded on the **Dhp 122** parallel (Sujato, CC0).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: do not belittle small good; drop by drop a great vessel fills.

## Source (Chinese, T210 — verbatim)

> 莫輕小善，以為無福，水滴雖微，漸盈大器。 (`zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0564c2101`)
