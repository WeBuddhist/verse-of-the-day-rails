# Day 47 — True renunciation (法句經 T210, 梵志品)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t210-true-renunciation]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); meaning grounded on the **Dhp 264–265** parallel (Sujato, CC0).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: not a shaved head or pious words but giving up all evil makes one a true renunciant.

## Source (Chinese, T210 — verbatim)

> 非剃為沙門，稱吉為梵志，謂能捨眾惡，是則為道人。 (`zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0572c0101`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — Not a shaved head makes one a monk, nor pious words a holy one; whoever gives up all evil, that one is truly of the Way.
- **zh** — 非剃為沙門，稱吉為梵志，謂能捨眾惡，是則為道人。

- **ne** — टाउको खौरेर कोही श्रमण हुँदैन, न मंगल-वचनले ब्राह्मण; जसले सबै पाप त्याग्छ, त्यही साँचो मार्गगामी हो।
