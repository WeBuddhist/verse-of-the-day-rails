# Day 40 — Concentration (佛遺教經 T389)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t389-concentration]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); from the Buddha's final instructions.
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: gather the mind on one point and nothing is beyond accomplishing.

## Source (Chinese, T389 — verbatim)

> 制之一處，無事不辦。 (`zh-bequeathed-teachings.md#^pT12p1111c1701`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **zh** — 制之一處，無事不辦。
