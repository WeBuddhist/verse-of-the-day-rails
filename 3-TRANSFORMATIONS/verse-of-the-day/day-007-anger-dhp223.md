# Day 7 — Overcoming Anger (Dhp 223)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/dhp-223]] · grounded in Sujato (CC0).
Meaning targeted: *conquer anger with non-anger, the unskillful person with goodness, the stingy with giving, the liar with truth* — four paired remedies, each fault met by its opposite virtue.
Flagged rendering choice: *akkodhena* = "non-anger" / "patience" / "kindness." This card uses **kindness** (Sujato) for warmth; keep the four-fold parallel.
