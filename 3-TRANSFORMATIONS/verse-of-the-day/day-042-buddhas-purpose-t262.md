# Day 42 — The Buddhas' one purpose (妙法蓮華經 T262)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t262-buddhas-purpose]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); Mahāyāna. The sūtra explains the "one great purpose" as opening all beings to awakening.
Short sentence, **quoted in full**. Meaning: the Buddhas appear for a single compassionate aim — to awaken beings.

## Source (Chinese, T262 — verbatim)

> 諸佛世尊，唯以一大事因緣故，出現於世。 (`zh-lotus-sutra.md#^pT09p0007a1505`)
