# Day 30 — Self-responsibility (法句經 T210, 愛身品)

Rail: [[2-RAILS/Verses/t210-self-responsibility]] · **own translation from the CBETA Chinese source** (CC BY-NC-SA); meaning grounded on the **Dhp 165** parallel (Sujato, CC0).
Short verse, **quoted in full**. Meaning: each bears the fruit of their own deeds; no one can bear it for you.

## Source (Chinese, T210 — verbatim)

> 惡自受罪，善自受福；亦各須熟，彼不自代。 (`zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0565c2101`)

## Renderings (one short line each)

- **en** — Evil brings its own penalty, good its own blessing; each ripens for you alone, and none can bear it in your place.
- **zh** — 惡自受罪，善自受福；亦各須熟，彼不自代。

- **ne** — नराम्रो कर्मको दण्ड आफैँ भोग्नुपर्छ, असलको फल पनि आफैँ; प्रत्येक आफ्नै लागि पाक्छ, कसैले तिम्रो सट्टामा भोग्दैन।
