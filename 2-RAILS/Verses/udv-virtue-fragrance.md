## Disambiguated Meaning
No flower's scent — not even sandalwood or aloeswood — travels against the wind; but the fragrance of a genuinely good person (*sappurisa*) travels in every direction, even against the wind. Buddha-spoken (Udānavarga). Theme: quiet, genuine virtue (*sīla* / ཚུལ་ཁྲིམས) doesn't need to be announced — it reaches everywhere on its own.
