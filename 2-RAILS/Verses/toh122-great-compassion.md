## Disambiguated Meaning
**Endowed with the awakening mind (bodhicitta / བྱང་ཆུབ་སེམས), cultivate the perception of great compassion (mahākaruṇā / སྙིང་རྗེ་ཆེན་པོ).** The sūtra's counsel: rest everything in bodhicitta and grow great compassion. Buddha-spoken Mahāyāna sūtra.
