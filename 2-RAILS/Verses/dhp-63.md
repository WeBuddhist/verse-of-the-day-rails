## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^63): a fool (*bāla*) who knows their own foolishness is wise at least in that; but one who imagines they are wise is the true fool. Buddha-spoken. Theme = self-knowledge / humility about what one does not know.
