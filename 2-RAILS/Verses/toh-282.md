## Disambiguated Meaning
"The Sūtra on the Threefold Training" (*bslab pa gsum gyi mdo*). Grounded in the Tibetan source (^p113): *This is ethics (ཚུལ་ཁྲིམས / sīla); this is concentration (ཏིང་ངེ་འཛིན / samādhi); this is wisdom (ཤེས་རབ / paññā).* Training in ethics leads to abiding long in concentration; training in concentration leads to gaining wisdom; training in wisdom frees the mind completely from greed, hatred, and delusion (འདོད་ཆགས་ / ཞེ་སྡང་ / གཏི་མུག). **Under the no-distillation + length rules the card does NOT render the whole progression (~230 chars, too long)** — it quotes one self-contained sentence, the path's fruit: ཤེས་རབ་ལ་གོམས་པར་བྱས་ན…རྣམ་པར་གྲོལ་བར་འགྱུར་རོ (*"Trained in wisdom, the mind is set completely free from greed, hatred, and delusion."*). The full progression is kept here as the meaning anchor only.
