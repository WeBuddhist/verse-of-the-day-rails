## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in Sujato (the Iti 22 gāthā, ^iti22-5-1+2): one should practice only good deeds (*puññāni* / *kusala*), whose happy outcome (*sukha*) stretches far ahead. The card renders this opening couplet of the verse in full (the verse goes on to name giving, conduct, and love as the three; the couplet stands complete on its own as "cultivate good — its happy fruit reaches far ahead"). Theme = wholesome action.
