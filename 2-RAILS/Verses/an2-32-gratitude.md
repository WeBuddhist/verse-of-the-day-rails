## Grounding
Self-contained sentence from the Aṅguttara (AN 2.32), quoted from Sujato (CC0). Pali prose Nikāya (beyond the Dhammapada).

## Disambiguated Meaning
**A true person (*sappurisa*) is grateful and thankful; gratitude (*kataññutā*) is the mark of the good** — only the virtuous truly know how to be grateful. Buddha-spoken.
