## Disambiguated Meaning
**Whoever wishes to be beautiful should cultivate patience (*kṣānti* / བཟོད་པ).** A gentle, memorable teaching that patience beautifies — anger disfigures, forbearance graces. Buddha-spoken Mahāyāna sūtra.
