## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (1-SOURCES/Translations/en-dhammapada-sujato.md#^50): Do not look to the faults of others (*paresaṁ vilomāni*) or to what they have or haven't done; look only to your own deeds — what you yourself have done and left undone (*attanova … katāni akatāni*). The turn is inward, away from judging others.

## Theme & Selection Notes
Theme: **self-reflection** — gentle, practical, very relatable (the impulse to judge others). Self-contained. A fresh, less-circulated verse.
