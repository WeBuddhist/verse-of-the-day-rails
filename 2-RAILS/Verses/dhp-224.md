## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^224): three simple practices — speak the truth (*sacca*), do not give way to anger (*akkodha*), and give when asked even a little (*dāna*) — by these three one draws near the gods. Theme = truthfulness (heading a small triad of everyday virtues). Short verse, rendered in full.
