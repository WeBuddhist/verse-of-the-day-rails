## Disambiguated Meaning
It is not matted hair or lineage that makes one noble (梵志 / *brāhmaṇa*); one who genuinely lives truth and Dhamma, and is pure, is the truly worthy one. Buddha-spoken (Dharmapada). Theme = nobility is inner conduct, not birth or outward marks.
