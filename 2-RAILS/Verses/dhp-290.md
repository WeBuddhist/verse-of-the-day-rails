## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^290): if, by giving up a limited happiness, one sees an abundant happiness, the wise let the lesser go for the sake of the greater. Buddha-spoken. Theme = wise priorities — trading a small good for a far greater one.
