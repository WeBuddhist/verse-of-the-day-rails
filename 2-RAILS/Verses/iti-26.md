## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in Sujato (^iti26-2-1, ^iti26-3-1): if beings knew, as the Buddha does, the fruit of giving and sharing (*dāna* / *saṁvibhāga*), they would not eat without first sharing, and the stain of stinginess (*macchariya*) would not occupy their minds. The card distils the warm, encouraging core: knowing the fruit of generosity, one delights to give. Theme = giving as joy, not loss.
