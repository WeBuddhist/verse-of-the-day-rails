## Source Text (CBETA — CC BY-NC-SA)
> 發大乘心，普濟一切。
> *(verbatim from `zh-eight-realizations.md#^pT17p0715b2301`, the eighth realization; the full line continues 願代眾生，受無量苦)*

## Grounding
Own modern rendering from the Chinese source; meaning cross-checked against standard translations of the Sūtra on the Eight Realizations. One self-contained clause, quoted in full. **Mahāyāna.**

## Disambiguated Meaning
The bodhisattva's aspiration: **give rise to the Great-Vehicle mind (bodhicitta, 大乘心) to bring relief to all beings universally** (普濟一切). The sūtra's eighth realization — the resolve to awaken for the sake of all.
