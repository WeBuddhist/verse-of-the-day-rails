## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^96): the mind is at peace, and so are the speech and deeds, of one rightly freed through true knowledge (*sammadaññā-vimutta*). Buddha-spoken. Theme = inner peace expressed in thought, word, and act.
