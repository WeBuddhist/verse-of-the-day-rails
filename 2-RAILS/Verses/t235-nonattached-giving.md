## Source Text (CBETA — CC BY-NC-SA)
> 菩薩於法，應無所住行於布施。
> *(verbatim from `zh-diamond-sutra.md#^pT08p0749a1201`; the line continues 不住色…布施 — giving without fixing on sights, sounds, etc.)*

## Grounding
Own modern rendering from the Chinese source; meaning cross-checked against standard Diamond Sūtra translations. One self-contained clause, quoted in full. **Mahāyāna.**

## Disambiguated Meaning
**Give without dwelling on anything at all** (應無所住行於布施) — practise generosity without attachment to giver, gift, or reward. The Diamond Sūtra's non-abiding (*apratiṣṭhita*) generosity. Not the over-exposed closing gāthā. Buddha-spoken.
