## Disambiguated Meaning

Grounded in the source verse and Sujato's rendering
(1-SOURCES/Translations/en-dhammapada-sujato.md#^5):

Hatred/enmity (*vera*) is **never**, at any time in this world, brought to rest
(*sammati*) by [responding with] hatred. It is brought to rest **only** by
non-hatred (*avera* — the absence of enmity, i.e. love / good-will). This is an
ancient, timeless principle (*sanantano dhammo*).

- *averena* is instrumental: "by means of non-hatred." Sujato renders it "by
  love"; the literal sense is "by not-hating." A modern rendering may use
  "love," "kindness," or "non-hatred" — the rail flags the choice rather than
  fixing it (that belongs to each language's `termbase.md`).
