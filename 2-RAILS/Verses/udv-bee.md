## Disambiguated Meaning
As a bee sips nectar and flies off without harming the flower's color or scent, so the sage (*muni* / ཐུབ་པ) moves through the village — taking only what is freely given, leaving no damage. Buddha-spoken (Udānavarga). Theme = living lightly, without burdening others.
