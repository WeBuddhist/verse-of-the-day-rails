## Source Text (CBETA — CC BY-NC-SA)
> 如可意華，色好無香，工語如是，不行無得。
> *(verbatim from `zh-dharmapada.md#^lgT04p0563a2301`, 華香品 / Flowers)*

## Grounding — Pali parallel
![[1-SOURCES/Translations/en-dhammapada-sujato.md#^51]]
Parallel to **Dhp 51** (a lovely but scentless flower = fine words that bear no fruit for one who does not act). Rendered from the Chinese source, cross-checked against Sujato (CC0).

## Disambiguated Meaning
Like a beautiful flower with no scent, well-spoken words (工語 / *subhāsita*) yield nothing for the one who does not act on them. Buddha-spoken (Dharmapada). Theme = words must be matched by deeds.
