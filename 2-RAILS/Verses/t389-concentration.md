## Source Text (CBETA — CC BY-NC-SA)
> 制之一處，無事不辦。
> *(verbatim from `zh-bequeathed-teachings.md#^pT12p1111c1701`, the Buddha's final instructions)*

## Grounding
Own modern rendering from the Chinese source; meaning cross-checked against standard translations of the Sūtra of Bequeathed Teachings. One self-contained sentence, quoted in full.

## Disambiguated Meaning
From the Buddha's last teaching: **gather the mind and fix it on one point, and there is nothing that cannot be accomplished** (制之一處，無事不辦). Concentration (one-pointedness) as the power behind every attainment.
