## Disambiguated Meaning
It is not the shaved head that makes one a śramaṇa (沙門), nor the recitation of auspicious words that makes one a brahmin (梵志); one who is able to give up all evil — this is truly a person of the Way. Buddha-spoken (Chinese Dharmapada). Theme: genuine renunciation/nobility is inner conduct (abandoning wrongdoing), not an outward mark or title.
