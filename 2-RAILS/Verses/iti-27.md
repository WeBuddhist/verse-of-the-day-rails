## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in Sujato (^iti27-2-1, ^iti27-3-1): *of all the grounds for making worldly merit, none is worth a sixteenth part of the heart's release by love* (mettā cetovimutti); surpassing them all, it *shines and glows and radiates* — as the moon outshines the stars. The card distils the moon/stars simile + the claim that loving-kindness is the most radiant good. Drop the autumn-sun and morning-star similes (repetition) for the daily card.
