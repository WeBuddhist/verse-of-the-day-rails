## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^131): all creatures long for happiness (*sukha*); if you harm them with the rod in search of your own happiness, you will not find happiness hereafter. Buddha-spoken. Theme = harmlessness (*ahiṁsā*), grounded in the shared wish to be happy.
