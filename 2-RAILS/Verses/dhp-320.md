## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^320): as a war-elephant endures the arrows shot at it in battle, so I will patiently endure harsh words (*ativākya*), for many people behave badly. The image: steadiness under provocation, not retaliation. Theme = patience / endurance (*khanti* / *titikkhā*).

## Theme & Selection Notes
Theme: **patience** — fresh, vivid, very relatable (enduring criticism/abuse). Pairs with the new *khanti* termbase row. Distinct from Day 7's *overcoming-anger* (Dhp 223): this is steady endurance, not active non-retaliation. License: CC0.
