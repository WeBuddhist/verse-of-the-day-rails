## Disambiguated Meaning

Grounded in the source verse and Sujato (1-SOURCES/Translations/en-dhammapada-sujato.md#^223):

Conquer anger with non-anger (*akkodhena jine kodhaṁ*); conquer the bad/unskillful person with goodness (*asādhuṁ sādhunā jine*); conquer the stingy with giving (*jine kadariyaṁ dānena*); conquer the liar with truth (*saccenālikavādinaṁ*). Four paired remedies, each overcoming a fault with its opposite virtue.

- *akkodhena* = "by non-anger" (instrumental). A modern rendering may use "patience," "kindness," or "non-anger" — flag for each termbase; keep the four-fold parallel structure.

## Theme & Selection Notes

Theme: **overcoming anger / responding with the opposite virtue.** Practical, memorable, four-fold structure. Self-contained; strong verse of the day.
