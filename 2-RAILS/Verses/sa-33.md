## Disambiguated Meaning
SĀ 33 ("Not Self") is the Saṁyuktāgama parallel to the Anattalakkhaṇa teaching. Grounded in Patton: *form is not the self; feeling, perception, volition, and consciousness likewise.* Seeing the five aggregates as not-self and not-mine, the noble disciple *grasps nothing … attaches to nothing*, and the mind is freed. The card distils the core line: the five aggregates are not the self; seeing this, one clings to nothing, and the heart is released.
