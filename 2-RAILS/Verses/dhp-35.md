## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^35): the mind (*citta*) is hard to hold back, flighty, alighting wherever it wishes; it is good to tame it, for a tamed mind brings happiness. Buddha-spoken. Theme = taming the mind. (Same felt-area as Day 5's Dhp 1 but a distinct verse and angle — repeats are fine, material deduped.)
