## Source Text (CBETA — CC BY-NC-SA)
> 諸佛世尊，唯以一大事因緣故，出現於世。
> *(verbatim from `zh-lotus-sutra.md#^pT09p0007a1505`, 方便品 / Skilful Means chapter; the sūtra unpacks the "one great purpose" as opening beings to awakening)*

## Grounding
Own modern rendering from the Chinese source; meaning cross-checked against standard Lotus Sūtra translations. One self-contained sentence, quoted in full. **Mahāyāna.**

## Disambiguated Meaning
**The Buddhas appear in the world for one great purpose alone** (一大事因緣) — which the sūtra explains as opening all beings to awakening (開示悟入佛之知見). The card quotes the "one great purpose" sentence; the meaning is the Buddhas' single compassionate aim.
