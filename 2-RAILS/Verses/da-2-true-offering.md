## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in the Chinese source (^pT01p0021a1103): the one who can **receive the teaching** (受法) and **put it into practice** (行法) — **this** is what is truly called **making offering to the Tathāgata** (供養如來). Real devotion is not scattering flowers or material honours but living the teaching. Buddha-spoken to Ānanda; a teaching that points the way, warmly reframing what devotion means.

- Rendering choice: 如來 (Tathāgata) → "the Buddha" for a lay, no-glossary audience (a reviewer may keep "the Tathāgata"). 供養 → "offering" (pūjā), the standard term. 受法 / 行法 → "receive / practise the teaching."
