## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^25): through effort (*uṭṭhāna*), diligence (*appamāda*), restraint (*saṁyama*), and self-control (*dama*), a wise person makes an island that no flood can wash over. The "island" = a refuge of character the troubles of life cannot submerge. Theme = effort / diligence building inner stability.
