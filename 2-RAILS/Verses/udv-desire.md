## Disambiguated Meaning
From desire (*kāma* / འདོད་པ) grief (*soka* / མྱ་ངན) is born, and fear (*bhaya* / འཇིགས་པ); for one wholly freed of desire there is no grief and no fear. Buddha-spoken (Udānavarga). Theme = freedom from craving.
