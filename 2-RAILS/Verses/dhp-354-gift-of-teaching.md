## Grounding
Grounded in source + Sujato (^354). The card quotes the verse's **first line** (a complete, self-contained statement); the verse continues with parallel lines on the taste/joy of the teaching and the ending of craving.

## Disambiguated Meaning
**The gift of the teaching (*dhammadāna*) surpasses all other gifts.** Sharing the Dharma is the highest generosity. Buddha-spoken.
