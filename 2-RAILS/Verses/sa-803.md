## Disambiguated Meaning
SĀ 803 is the Saṁyuktāgama parallel to the Pali Ānāpānasati teaching. The card distils the **practical pith**, not the full 16-step list (too long/technical for a daily card): *cultivate mindfulness of breathing — aware of the in-breath as in-breath, the out-breath as out-breath; practised often, body and mind grow calm* (身心俱得安止). The verse meets a restless or anxious reader with a concrete, doable practice.
