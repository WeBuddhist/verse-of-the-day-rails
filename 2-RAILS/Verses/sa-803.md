## Disambiguated Meaning
SĀ 803 is the Saṁyuktāgama parallel to the Pali Ānāpānasati teaching. **Under the no-distillation rule the card does NOT compress the 16-step list** — it quotes one self-contained sentence of the Buddha's words (Patton): *"If a monk cultivates the mindfulness of breathing and cultivates it often, he'll attain calmness of body and mind"* (身心俱得安止). The verse meets a restless or anxious reader with a concrete, doable practice.
