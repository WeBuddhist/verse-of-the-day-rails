## Disambiguated Meaning
Grounded in source + Sujato (^81): as the wind cannot move a solid mass of rock, so blame and praise (*nindā* / *pasaṁsā*) do not stir the wise. Theme = equanimity — steadiness amid others' judgements. Short verse, rendered in full.
